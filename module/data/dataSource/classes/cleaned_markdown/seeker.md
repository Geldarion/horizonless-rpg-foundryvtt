# Maneuvers

At 1st Rank, you learn three maneuvers, and you learn additional ones as
you gain additional Ranks as shown in the Class Overview Table. During
the Retrain downtime activity, you may redo the selection of all of your
known maneuvers.

# Seeker Pursuit

*When you've got an itch to scratch, start by finding the right angle.*

At 1st Rank, you choose a Seeker Pursuit from which you draw your skills
and tactics. You gain features from this chosen Seeker Pursuit at 1st,
4th, and 8th Rank.

# Alacrity I

*The setup and takedown, all in one fluid motion.*

At 1st Rank, whenever you take the Stagger action or inflict
staggered, you may enter *alacrity*. Your chosen Seeker
Pursuit provides an alternate way to enter alacrity. Entering alacrity
multiple times does not stack. At the start and end of combat, you exit
alacrity if you have it.

While you have alacrity, your Speed is increased by 10 feet. Some
features require you to exit alacrity, such as **Cunning Strike**
described below.

## Cunning Strike

When you hit a creature with a spell or weapon attack, you may exit
alacrity to perform a *cunning strike* if the attack satisfies either
the requirements listed below:

- **Advantage.** You have advantage or greater on the attack check.

- **Nearby Ally.** An ally is within 5 feet of the target of the attack.

Cunning strikes add **Xd8 damage** to the attack's damage roll, where X
is your Seeker Rank. You may perform a cunning strike at most once per
round.

# Seeker Tricks I

*With a sleeve full of backup plans, you have got what it takes to
finish the job.*

At 2nd Rank, select one Seeker Trick feature of your choosing from the
list below. During the Retrain downtime activity, you may redo the
selection of all of your chosen Seeker Tricks.

As a free action once during each of your turns, you may use one of your
Seeker Trick features.

### Getaway

2nd Rank Seeker Trick

You cleanse the grappled and slowed
conditions from a target ally within your Reach.

### Leg Sweep

2nd Rank Seeker Trick

You perform a free Trip action.

### Surveil

2nd Rank Seeker Trick

You perform a free Analyze action.

# Backflip

*Going for style is your style.*

At 2nd Rank, you gain the **Backflip** Seeker Trick described below:

### Backflip

2nd Rank Seeker Trick

You travel up to half your Speed. During this movement, you may freely
travel through spaces occupied by hostile creatures.

# Gambit I

*On your signal, the plan is in motion.*\
At 3rd Rank, once per rest, as an action at the start of your turn, you
may allow an additional ally that has not yet taken their turn this
round to also take their turn. This may exceed the limit of two
creatures taking their turn together.

When you or an ally uses a feature that references their initiative
partner, they choose which ally to which the feature will apply (the
chosen ally must be taking their turn).

# Close Call

*Nimbly, you cheat certain death.*

At 3rd Rank, as a reaction when you are dealt damage from an attack, you
may halve the damage dealt. As a reaction before you make a basic
**Reflex** saving throw, you may cause a failure to only deal half
damage to you and a success to have no damage be dealt to you.

# Ability Improvement

At 3rd, 6th, and 9th Rank, you gain an Ability Improvement. Each time
you gain an Ability Improvement, distribute three permanent +1 increases
to your ability modifiers. You may increase the same ability modifier
multiple times.

The maximum ability modifier that may be achieved is limited by your
character's Level. The maximum is +5 at Level 3, increases to +6 at
Level 6, and finally becomes +7 at Level 9.

# Exploration

At 4th, 7th, and 10th Rank, you gain an Exploration with Seeker being
the exploring Class. Each time you gain an Exploration, you gain the
following benefit:

- **Bonus Rank.** You choose any Class other than Seeker and gain one
  Rank in the chosen Class.

# Seeker Pursuit Feature

At 4th Rank, you gain a feature for your Seeker Pursuit.

# Alacrity II

*Controlled chaos is ripe with opportunity.*

At 5th Rank, the Speed bonus you gain from alacrity becomes 20 feet.
When you make a Stagger special attack, the next weapon or spell attack
check made against the target this turn has its critical hit threshold
reduced by 1.

# Seeker Tricks II

*Always prepared with the tools of the trade.*

At 5th Rank, select one of the following Seeker Trick features from the
list below or one listed at an earlier Rank. During the Retrain downtime
activity, you may redo the selection of all of your chosen Seeker
Tricks.

### Bola

5th Rank Seeker Trick

You apply slowed with Recoverable to a target within 15
feet of you.

### Cover

5th Rank Seeker Trick

You grant a free Dodge action to a target ally within your Reach.

### Smoke Bomb

5th Rank Seeker Trick

You grant a free Vanish action to yourself or an ally within your Reach.

# Gambit II

*Every branch of the plan has been mapped out. We move as one.*

At 6th Rank, whenever you use **Gambit**, all allies taking their turn,
including yourself, may add their Speed to their movement pool.

# Flourish I

*Even your backup plans have backup plans.*

At 6th Rank, once per rest during your turn, you may allow yourself to
use an additional Seeker Trick this turn.

# Ability Improvement

At 6th Rank, you gain an Ability Improvement. Each time you gain an
Ability Improvement, distribute three permanent +1 increases to your
ability modifiers. You may increase the same ability modifier multiple
times.

The maximum ability modifier that may be achieved is limited by your
character's Level. The maximum is +5 at Level 3, increases to +6 at
Level 6, and finally becomes +7 at Level 9.

# Razor Sharp

*After having come this far, you've got nerves of steel and a sixth
sense for things that can go wrong.*

At 7th Rank, you gain an additional reaction at the start of each of
your turns.

# Exploration

At 7th Rank, you gain an Exploration with Seeker being the exploring
Class. Each time you gain an Exploration, you gain the following
benefit:

- **Bonus Rank.** You choose any Class other than Seeker and gain one
  Rank in the chosen Class.

# Seeker Tricks III

*Call the shot. Set the pace. Make the play.*

At 8th Rank, select one of the following Seeker Trick features from the
list below or one listed at an earlier Rank. During the Retrain downtime
activity, you may redo the selection of all of your chosen Seeker
Tricks.

### Coordinate

8th Rank Seeker Trick

You grant a free Anticipate action to a target ally within your Reach.

### Double Tap

8th Rank Seeker Trick

If you have scored a critical hit this turn, the next attack you make
this turn that hits also scores a critical hit.

### Snap Out

8th Rank Seeker Trick

You cleanse the incapacitated and stunned
conditions from a target ally within your Reach.

# Seeker Pursuit Feature

At 8th Rank, you gain a feature for your Seeker Pursuit.

# Alacrity III

*Plot an attack so sudden, it leaves them reeling.*

At 9th Rank, the Speed bonus you gain from alacrity becomes 30 feet.
Additionally, whenever you perform a cunning strike, the target of the
attack is disoriented until the end of their next turn.

# Flourish II

*Double or nothing.*

At 9th Rank, you may now use **Flourish** twice per rest.

# Ability Improvement

At 9th Rank, you gain an Ability Improvement. Each time you gain an
Ability Improvement, distribute three permanent +1 increases to your
ability modifiers. You may increase the same ability modifier multiple
times.

The maximum ability modifier that may be achieved is limited by your
character's Level. The maximum is +5 at Level 3, increases to +6 at
Level 6, and finally becomes +7 at Level 9.

# Gambit III

*"Now this is where you come in."*

At 10th Rank, whenever you use **Gambit**, you may designate an ally.
The designated ally gains advantage on the next ability check they make
this turn.

# Exploration

At 10th Rank, you gain an Exploration with Seeker being the exploring
Class. Each time you gain an Exploration, you gain the following
benefit:

- **Bonus Rank.** You choose any Class other than Seeker and gain one
  Rank in the chosen Class.

# Alacrity IV

*Pure speed and sleight of hand.*

At 11th Rank, the Speed bonus you gain from alacrity becomes 40 feet.
Whenever you hit with a Stagger special attack and inflict
staggered, you may then deal **1 physical damage** (you
choose which type) to the target and exit alacrity to perform a cunning
strike regardless of the requirements.

# Seeker Tricks IV

*Slow is smooth, smooth is fast.*

At 11th Rank, select one of the following Seeker Trick features from the
list below or one listed at an earlier Rank. During the Retrain downtime
activity, you may redo the selection of all of your chosen Seeker
Tricks.

### Exit Strategy

11th Rank Seeker Trick

You exit alacrity to cleanse all conditions from yourself or a target
ally within your Reach.

### Go Time

11th Rank Seeker Trick

You exit alacrity to grant an additional action this turn to an
initiative partner within your Reach.

### Sucker Punch

11th Rank Seeker Trick

You exit alacrity to cause a target creature within your Reach already
suffering from the disoriented condition to make a
**Fortitude** saving throw against your Maneuver DC. On a failure, the
target is stunned until the end of their next turn.

# Masterstroke

*The game was never theirs to play.*

At 12th Rank, once per long rest as a free action at any point, you may
dramatically reveal that everything was all according to plan, and have
retroactively made preparations to account for this very moment. You, or
an ally of your choice in an advantageous position, may make a weapon
attack check, use a feature or item, or cast a spell with any casting
time. If the feature or spell imposes a saving throw, the target(s)
automatically fail(s). If it's an attack check, it automatically hits
and scores a critical hit.

# Final Reach

When you reach character Level 12, you compute your *Total Rank*, which
is calculated as:

###### The sum of all of your Class Ranks

If your Total Rank is less than 16 when you reach Level 12, then for
each deficient Rank, you may choose one of the benefits below to modify
a feature granted by one of your Class Ranks:

- **Maneuvers.** You learn two additional maneuvers.

- **Battlecasting.** You gain a maneuver, an additional Spell Point, and
  an additional prepared spell for that Class.

- **Spellcasting.** You gain three additional Spell Points and three
  additional prepared spells for that Class.

Seeker Pursuits

# Seeker Pursuit: Nighteye

*Deep shadows cut into the murky urban alleys; the cruel figure of a
corrupt guard falls lifelessly to the ground in front of their would-be
victims. A robber baron's torch flickers in the depths of his empty
vault, horror creeping onto his lips. Many put faith in an unseen
guardian, you know it is not misplaced.*

## Unseen

*Time to get to work.*

At 1st Rank, you have a pool of 2d6 called your *unseen dice pool*. As a
free action when you make a Vanish check, you may expend an unseen die
to add it to the total. When you score a critical hit, you regain one
expended unseen die. At the end of every rest, your unseen dice pool is
refilled.

## Cloaked Threat

*Nothing moves faster than what you cannot see.*

At 1st Rank, once during your turn as a free action, if no hostile
creatures **perceive** you, enter alacrity.

## Legerdemain

*Ignore the hand behind the curtain.*

At 4th Rank, you learn the **Legerdemain** Seeker Trick described below:

### Legerdemain

4th Rank Nighteye Seeker Trick

Your next action or multiaction this turn doesn't cause you to become
**perceived**.

## Night's Rook

*Take all of your pieces off the board.*

At 8th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

- **Shadow Conspiracy.** Your unseen dice pool becomes 4d6, and they can
  now be spent on any Vanish check made by a target ally within 60 feet
  of you.

- **Speed of Darkness.** While you have alacrity, all Search checks
  against you incur disadvantage.

# Seeker Pursuit: Scoundrel

*It doesn't matter what hand you were dealt, whether you cheated at
every turn and lied through gritted, bloodied teeth. It doesn't matter
that they hate you, or that they plot behind your back. You're still
here, and whatever unfinished business still waits for you will come to
a close. Trust.*

## Head on a Swivel

*Only fools play by the rules.*

At 1st Rank, whenever you end your turn with alacrity, you gain your
Half Guard AC until the start of your next turn.

## Shoot First

*...ask questions later.*

At 1st Rank, when you begin combat, you enter alacrity.

## Discombobulate

*Discombobulate.*

At 4th Rank, you learn the **Discombobulate** Seeker Trick described
below:

### Discombobulate

4th Rank Scoundrel Seeker Trick

You make a special melee attack using your STR or DEX against a target
creature within your Reach. If it hits, the target is
disoriented until the end of their next turn.

## Another Round

*Keep 'em coming.*

At 8th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

- **Bob and Weave.** Whenever you end your turn with alacrity, you may
  take a free Dodge action.

- **Distract.** Your **Discombobulate** special attack is always made
  against the target's Base AC.

# Seeker Pursuit: Spell Thief

*Within the annals of arcane history lies mystery shrouded in the
margins of works held by the masters. Endless knowledge, the keys to
every door that has ever existed, untold power and acclaim ready to be
discovered and rediscovered by countless magi practicing in their dark
towers, their hidden groves, austere temples and wretched swamps. These
secrets have terrible prices, but that doesn't mean you have to pay
them...*

## Case Arcana

*Each discipline of magic has its quirks, but they crack all the same.*

At 1st Rank, choose three cantrips, each from any spell list. You may
cast those chosen cantrips using your INT, WIS, or CHA as your
spellcasting modifier. During the Retrain downtime activity, you may
redo your selection of chosen cantrips.

Once per rest, as a reaction when a target creature within 60 feet of
you casts a baseline 1st Circle spell, you may *capture* that spell.
Until the end of your next rest, you may cast the captured spell once at
1st Circle using your INT, WIS, or CHA as your spellcasting modifier.

## Spellrush

*Fraying spell byproducts is just the kick you need.*

At 1st Rank, whenever you cast a spell, you enter alacrity.

## Misdirect

*A secret's life can be short-lived, but never unimportant.*

At 4th Rank, you learn the **Misdirect** Seeker Trick described below:

### Misdirect

4th Rank Spell Thief Seeker Trick

You cast a cantrip with a casting time of one action (the spell is cast
as a free action).

## Hack Magic

*What's yours, now mine.*

At 8th Rank, once per long rest, when you use **Case Arcana** to capture
a spell, you may capture any spell. Until the end of your next rest, you
may cast the captured spell once at the Circle it was cast using your
INT, WIS, or CHA as your spellcasting modifier.
