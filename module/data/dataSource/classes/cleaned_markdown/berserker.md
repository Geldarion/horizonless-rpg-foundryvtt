**Maximum Hit Points (HP) gained per Rank:** 9

**Maximum Stamina (ST) gained per Rank:** 9

# Maneuvers

At 1st Rank, you learn three maneuvers, and you learn additional ones as
you gain additional Ranks as shown in the Class Overview Table. During
the Retrain downtime activity, you may redo the selection of all of your
known maneuvers.

# Berserker Mettle

*All souls hold a great power within. Unleash yours and begin to put the
heavens on notice.*

At 1st Rank, you choose a Berserker Mettle, the origin of your
perseverance and resilience. You gain features from your chosen
Berserker Mettle at 1st, 4th, and 8th Rank.

# Shockwave I

*"This attack has all your names written on it!"*

At 1st Rank, immediately after you hit with a melee weapon attack, you
may create an *aftershock* originating from the target. When your
aftershock is created, you deal **damage** equal to your STR (minimum
of 1) to all creatures and objects other than you within a 5 foot radius
sphere. The type of damage dealt by your aftershock may be determined
either by the type of damage dealt by your weapon attacks or by your
Berserker Mettle.

# Wrath

*Your foes bleed and break, and the fires within burn brighter with
every drop they lose.*

At 2nd Rank, whenever you deal damage to one or more Tier I or greater
creatures with your aftershock, you gain 1 *wrath charge* for each of
those creatures. Additionally, whenever you succeed on a STR combat
check, you gain 1 *wrath charge*.

While you have 1 or more wrath charges, you have DB 5 against all
damage, and at the start of each of your turns, you lose 1 wrath charge.
This DB becomes 10/15/20 at 4th/7th/10th Rank, respectively.

# Wrath Evolutions I

*Forge your weapons of fury in the fires of your soul.*

At 2nd Rank, select one Wrath Evolution feature of your choosing from
the list below, which enhances the capabilities of your **Wrath**
feature. During the Retrain downtime activity, you may redo the
selection of all of your chosen Wrath Evolutions.

### Bruise

2nd Rank Berserker Wrath Evolution

While you have at least 2 wrath charges, if a basic combat action,
maneuver, or Berserker feature attempts to inflict a condition on a
creature, and the condition is not applied for any reason, the creature
becomes staggered instead.

### Quake

2nd Rank Berserker Wrath Evolution

You may expend 1 wrath charge when you create an aftershock to double
the damage dealt by the aftershock.

### Trash Talk

2nd Rank Berserker Wrath Evolution

You may expend 1 wrath charge when you hit with a melee weapon attack to
cause the target to become provoked by you until the start
of your next turn.

# Legion of One I

*They said "you and what army?". Little did they know I am worth two
armies.*

At 3rd Rank, when you make a Grapple, Pin, Shove, Trip, or Wreck An
Object STR combat check, you may immediately repeat the STR combat check
against all creatures within your Reach other than the original target
as a free action. If you do, use the total of the original STR combat
check for all others rather than rolling each of them. Alternatively,
when you take the Throw action, you may throw all creatures you have
grappled.

You may use **Legion of One** twice per rest.

# Taunt I

*A few choice words, a rude gesture, and a smug grin are enough to make
any creature raving mad.*

At 3rd Rank, as a free action once per rest when a hostile combat team
takes initiative, you may choose two hostile creatures who have not yet
taken their turn this round. The chosen creatures must take their turn
and are provoked by you until the end of the turn.

# Ability Improvement

At 3rd, 6th, and 9th Rank, you gain an Ability Improvement. Each time
you gain an Ability Improvement, distribute three permanent +1 increases
to your ability modifiers. You may increase the same ability modifier
multiple times.

The maximum ability modifier that may be achieved is limited by your
character's Level. The maximum is +5 at Level 3, increases to +6 at
Level 6, and finally becomes +7 at Level 9.

# Exploration

At 4th, 7th, and 10th Rank, you gain an Exploration with Berserker being
the exploring Class. Each time you gain an Exploration, you gain the
following benefit:

- **Bonus Rank.** You choose any Class other than Berserker and gain one
  Rank in the chosen Class.

# Berserker Mettle Feature

At 4th Rank, you gain a feature for your Berserker Mettle.

# Shockwave II

*Throw your whole being into every swing, and force your foes to buckle
under its weight.*

At 5th Rank, the radius of your aftershock becomes 10 feet.
Additionally, you may expend 3 wrath charges when you create an
aftershock to cause all creatures who took damage from it to become
staggered.

# Wrath Evolutions II

*Ignite your rage and unleash retribution.*

At 5th Rank, select one of the following Wrath Evolution features from
the list below or one listed at an earlier Rank. During the Retrain
downtime activity, you may redo the selection of all of your chosen
Wrath Evolutions.

### Bloodrage

5th Rank Berserker Wrath Evolution

You gain 1 wrath charge each time your **Wrath** DB breaks.

### Bodyslam

5th Rank Berserker Wrath Evolution

While you have at least 4 wrath charges, attacks against creatures you
have grappled gain advantage.

### Lash Out

5th Rank Berserker Wrath Evolution

While you have at least 4 wrath charges, as a reaction when you are
attacked, you may make a weapon attack against the attacker.

# Taunt II

*"What's the matter, did I upset you? Take your best shot, I won't even
feel it."*

At 6th Rank, when you use **Taunt**, your **Wrath** DB is increased by
10 × the number of targets chosen for your **Taunt** feature until the
end of the current turn.

# Legion of One II

*"Don't worry, I brought enough punishment for everyone."*

At 6th Rank, you may now use **Legion of One** three times per rest.

# Ability Improvement

At 6th Rank, you gain an Ability Improvement. Each time you gain an
Ability Improvement, distribute three permanent +1 increases to your
ability modifiers. You may increase the same ability modifier multiple
times.

The maximum ability modifier that may be achieved is limited by your
character's Level. The maximum is +5 at Level 3, increases to +6 at
Level 6, and finally becomes +7 at Level 9.

# Charge Into Action

*The only thing between us is the beef I have with you.*

At 7th Rank, once during your first turn of combat as a free action, you
may travel up to double your Speed in a straight line. This movement
must begin by traveling towards a hostile creature.

# Exploration

At 7th Rank, you gain an Exploration with Berserker being the exploring
Class. Each time you gain an Exploration, you gain the following
benefit:

- **Bonus Rank.** You choose any Class other than Berserker and gain one
  Rank in the chosen Class.

# Wrath Evolutions III

*Your foes begin to see the feral flame flicker behind your eyes.*

At 8th Rank, select one of the following Wrath Evolution features from
the list below or one listed at an earlier Rank. During the Retrain
downtime activity, you may redo the selection of all of your chosen
Wrath Evolutions.

### Evoke Panic

8th Rank Berserker Wrath Evolution

While you have at least 6 wrath charges, hostile creatures within 30
feet of you that are frightened are also
provoked by you.

### Furious Brawn

8th Rank Berserker Wrath Evolution

While you have at least 6 wrath charges, you gain advantage on STR
combat and effort checks.

### Note Disrespect

8th Rank Berserker Wrath Evolution

You gain 1 wrath charge each time a condition you have applied to a
hostile creature is removed. You may gain a maximum of 3 wrath charges
per round using this feature.

# Berserker Mettle Feature

At 8th Rank, you gain a feature for your Berserker Mettle.

# Shockwave III

*Hit them hard and with all the disrespect you can muster.*

At 9th Rank, the radius of your aftershock becomes 15 feet.
Additionally, you may expend 5 wrath charges when you create an
aftershock to cause all creatures who took damage from it to become
provoked by you until the start of your next turn.

# Legion of One III

*It doesn't matter the number of foes you face, all within your reach
will know your might.*

At 9th Rank, you may now use **Legion of One** four times per rest.

# Ability Improvement

At 9th Rank, you gain an Ability Improvement. Each time you gain an
Ability Improvement, distribute three permanent +1 increases to your
ability modifiers. You may increase the same ability modifier multiple
times.

The maximum ability modifier that may be achieved is limited by your
character's Level. The maximum is +5 at Level 3, increases to +6 at
Level 6, and finally becomes +7 at Level 9.

# Taunt III

*"I have survived worse than this, worse than you. Test me if you want,
you will shatter like all those who came before you."*

At 10th Rank, when choosing hostile creatures for your **Taunt**
feature, you may instead choose all hostile creatures that are suffering
from a condition who have not yet taken their turn this round. This may
exceed the limit of two creatures taking their turn together.

When a creature taking their turn uses a feature that references their
initiative partner, they choose which ally to which the feature will
apply.

# Exploration

At 10th Rank, you gain an Exploration with Berserker being the exploring
Class. Each time you gain an Exploration, you gain the following
benefit:

- **Bonus Rank.** You choose any Class other than Berserker and gain one
  Rank in the chosen Class.

# Leap Into Action

*I'm about to make an impact crater big enough to bury these fools.*

At 11th Rank, when you use **Charge Into Action**, you gain advanced
Jumping mobility during the movement. Afterwards, you may create an
aftershock originating from you as a free action.

# Wrath Evolutions IV

*Stoke your anger into a blaze strong enough to create a second dawn.*

At 11th Rank, select one of the following Wrath Evolution features from
the list below or one listed at an earlier Rank. During the Retrain
downtime activity, you may redo the selection of all of your chosen
Wrath Evolutions.

### Mutual Destruction

11th Rank Berserker Wrath Evolution

You may expend 7 wrath charges to cause all creatures who take damage
from your next aftershock to gain all conditions from other creatures
who also took damage from it.

### Shock and Awe

11th Rank Berserker Wrath Evolution

While you have at least 8 wrath charges, once per turn when you use
**Shockwave**, you may create an additional number of aftershocks
originating from the target of the attack equal to the number of
conditions affecting them.

### Too Angry to Die

11th Rank Berserker Wrath Evolution

You may expend 7 wrath charges as a free action when you are dealt
damage to negate all of it.

# Seismic Smash

*Unleash all your might into an attack so devastating the world cries,
the heavens quake, and your foes' lives flash before their eyes.*

At 12th Rank, once per rest, when you create an aftershock, you may have
its radius become 30 feet and cause all creatures who took damage from
it to become stunned until the start of your next turn.

# Final Reach

When you reach character Level 12, you compute your *Total Rank*, which
is calculated as:

###### The sum of all of your Class Ranks

If your Total Rank is less than 16 when you reach Level 12, then for
each deficient Rank, you may choose one of the benefits below to modify
a feature granted by one of your Class Ranks:

- **Maneuvers.** You learn two additional maneuvers.

- **Battlecasting.** You gain a maneuver, an additional Spell Point, and
  an additional prepared spell for that Class.

- **Spellcasting.** You gain three additional Spell Points and three
  additional prepared spells for that Class.

Berserker Mettles

# Berserker Mettle: Coldfury

*Underneath the cool facade is a frigid core of rage and focus. The
fusion of primal fear and isolation forces me to crystalize survival. In
time, they will fall to my wrath, plead for their lives as the walls
close in, and find no mercy from the arctic predator in my gaze.*

## Chilling Presence

*I am the winter of death that comes.*

At 1st Rank, while hostile creatures are within your Reach, they are
slowed.

## Icicle Wave

*As the air itself slows in anticipation, the mist collects to form the
barrier of my domain.*

At 1st Rank, your aftershocks may deal **cold damage**. When you create
an aftershock, you may form a thin wall of solid ice along the entire
boundary of the area. The wall is 1 foot thick and 10 feet tall. The
wall has 5 HP, an AC of 10, and 5 Toughness. Creatures are incapable of
climbing the wall without advanced Climbing mobility.

The entire wall is destroyed if any part of the wall is destroyed, and
all creatures within 5 feet of the wall are dealt **1d6 cold damage**.
This damage roll becomes 2d6/3d6/4d6 at 4th/7th/10th Rank, respectively.

## Freezing Wrath

*Gaining my attention will freeze you in your tracks.*

At 4th Rank, you may expend 2 wrath charges as a free action to cause a
target that travels into your Reach to become stuck. This
stuck condition gains the **Recoverable** condition trait,
allowing an action to end it.

## Frostbite

*They fumble in the cold, their sapped energy depriving them of
options.*

At 8th Rank, when a creature begins their turn within your Reach, they
take 1 fewer action this turn.

# Berserker Mettle: Flamewrought

*My path is littered with the embers of loss, injustice, and personal
insult. My anger burns brightly and fuels the vengeance my enemies have
wrought upon themselves.*

## Trial by Fire

*I mete out justice, branding their iniquity with consecrated
conflagration.*

At 1st Rank, as a free action once during each of your turns, you may
either become ablaze or cleanse yourself of the condition.
While you are ablaze, you gain the following benefits:

- **Hellfire.** The DB damage reduction cost is doubled for **fire
  damage** you deal with weapon attacks or from your
  ablaze conditions.

- **Vengeful Blaze.** As a reaction when you or an ally within 15 feet
  of you takes damage, you may cause the source of the damage to become
  ablaze.

## Ignition Wave

*Flames burst forth, too hot to contain.*

At 1st Rank, your aftershocks may deal **fire damage**. You may also
cause all creatures who take any type of damage from your aftershocks to
become ablaze.

## Burning Wrath

*You shall burn as I, together wreathed in the inferno.*

At 4th Rank, you may expend 2 wrath charges as a free action when you
are attacked to lower the attack's critical hit threshold by 2. If the
attack hits, any damage dealt to you by the attack is also dealt to the
attacker as **fire damage**.

## Living Thermite

*Pour your volcanic ire upon them; bury them in ash and igneous
retribution.*

At 8th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

- **Eternal Flame.** When you are about to burn a Resolve when taking
  damage immediately after using **Burning Wrath**, you may instead
  expend 2 wrath charges to gain the effects of burning a Resolve.

- **Thermal Cascade.** When a target takes damage from your
  ablaze conditions, they inflict ablaze to
  all creatures within 5 feet of them.

# Berserker Mettle: Siegebreaker

*Thunder crashes amidst the pouring rain as I glare down their feeble
defenses. Their foolish faith is in their walls of matchwood. The first
hammer to strike the wedge is the terror my name holds, followed by the
pounding of my fists through their blockade.They jump at the lightning
above, but their fear should be directed towards the storm below. To
me.*

## Power Through

*Don't get in my way.*

At 1st Rank, damage dealt by your aftershocks to objects is increased by
10. Once per turn when you break an object, you add your Speed to your
movement pool.

## Hurricane Wave

*I am the storm after the calm, and the hour of your demise is rapidly
approaching.*

At 1st Rank, your aftershocks may deal **bludgeoning damage**. When you
deal damage to a creature with an aftershock, that creature becomes
*shattered* until the start of their next turn. While shattered, their
AC is reduced by one guard step (their AC is either their Full Guard AC,
Half Guard AC, or Base AC).

## Implosive Wrath

*With a crackle of cataclysmic force, their strength is nothing against
the storm.*

At 4th Rank, you may expend 2 wrath charges as an action to cause any
number of target creatures within 30 feet of you to make a **Poise**
saving throw against your Maneuver DC. On a failure, a creature is
pulled to a target unoccupied space within your Reach. At the end of
your turn, you push any creatures within your Reach 15 feet away from
you. Creatures that are pushed in this way fall prone.

## Battering Ram

*Your fortifications fall to my fist.*

At 8th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

- **Blastsmith.** The aftershock damage bonus of **Power Through**
  against objects becomes 15.

- **Shattered Ankles.** Creatures that start their turn shattered are
  slowed until the end of their turn.
