# Occult Battlecasting

As a Hexblade, you are able to prepare and cast spells from the Occult
spell list:

- **Spells Prepared.** At 1st Rank, you may prepare 4 spells for this
  Class at the end of each long rest. You may cast those prepared spells
  using this feature.

- **Class Circle Cap.** Each spell you prepare may have a maximum
  baseline Circle of 1st Circle.

- **Heightening.** When you cast spells using this feature, you may
  heighten spells up to your Class Circle Cap.

- **Spell Points.** At 1st Rank, you gain 2 Spell Points. When you cast
  spells using this feature, you must expend Spell Points equal to the
  Circle of the spell. Spell Points are regained at the end of each long
  rest.

- **Spellcasting Modifier.** Your spellcasting modifier when casting
  spells using this feature is CHA.

  - **Your Spell Attack Bonus** = your CHA + your Tier Bonus

  - **Your Spell Save DC** = 8 + your CHA + your Tier Bonus

  - **Spellcasting Checks** made with spells prepared for this class use
    your CHA.

- **Signature Spell.** Your designated signature spell may be one of the
  1st Circle or higher spells prepared for this feature.

- **Higher Ranks.** At higher Ranks, the spells prepared, Spell Points,
  and Class Circle Cap all improve as described in the Class Overview
  Table.

# Hexblade Lure

*Toeing the line between uncertain doom and immeasurable power, you
brave the dangerous path and make it your own. You have everything to
lose and everything to gain.*

At 1st Rank, you choose a Hexblade Lure: a false promise that you aim to
make true. You gain features from this chosen Hexblade Lure at 1st, 4th,
and 8th Rank.

# Doomglyphs

*You begin to tap into perilous forces beyond your control and bargain
with voices that tempt you from beyond the reaches of obscurity.*

At 1st Rank, you gain access to *doomglyphs*, which increase your *doom
DC* and cause you to make a *doom check* immediately afterwards. You
learn one doomglyph from your Hexblade Lure, as well as the **Hungering
Strike** doomglyph described below*.*

### Hungering Strike

Hexblade Doomglyph

When you make an attack check, you may increase your doom DC by 3 to add
+3 to the check's total. After the attack, you make a doom check.

## Doom Checks

Your *doom DC* begins at 0 and can increase to a maximum of 20. *Doom
checks* are flat checks against your doom DC. On a success, nothing
happens. On a failure, your doom DC reverts to 0 and you *awaken* a
*spirit of violence*, which has the following properties:

- **Object.** The spirit of violence is a Small object with 5 HP, 15 AC,
  and 5 Toughness.

- **Mobility.** The spirit of violence has advanced Hovering mobility.

- **Speed.** The spirit of violence has a Speed equal to your Speed.

- **Hostile.** The spirit of violence is controlled by the GM and
  awakens 30 feet away from you at a location of the GM's choice.

- **Lured.** The spirit of violence is granted additional features by
  your Hexblade Lure.

- **Reaper.** At the end of each of your turns, the GM may have it
  travel up to its Speed and then, if it is within 5 feet of you, it may
  make a *reaper* melee special attack against you. Reaper attacks have
  a +6 attack bonus and deal **5 injuring necrotic damage** that ignores
  DBs on a hit.

- **Combative.** If a spirit of violence is awakened, combat begins with
  only your combat team if you are not already in combat.

At higher character Levels, the statistics of the spirit of violence
improves as shown in the table below:

# Hex

*A malignant mist reaches out and shares your misery with your foes.*

At 2nd Rank, as an action, you may *bestow* your *hex* on a target
within 60 feet of you. The subject remains *hexed* by you until the end
of combat, it burns a Resolve, or you bestow your hex on a different
subject. You cannot bestow your hex on a subject that is currently hexed
by you.

When you hit your hexed subject with an attack, you may add **1d8
necrotic damage** to the damage roll. This 1d8 is your *hex die*, and it
becomes 2d8/3d8/4d8 at 4th/7th/10th Rank, respectively.

# Hexwrought Curses I

*Sparks of electric misfortune hone in on those you've primed for their
demise.*

At 2nd Rank, select one of the following Hexwrought Curse features from
the list below, which enhance the capabilities of your **Hex** feature.
During the Retrain downtime activity, you may redo the selection of all
of your chosen Hexwrought Curses.

### Chilling Dusk

2nd Rank Hexblade Hexwrought Curse

When you hit your hexed subject with a melee weapon attack, it becomes
slowed until the start of your next turn.

### Reap Vitality

2nd Rank Hexblade Hexwrought Curse

When your hexed subject reaches 0 HP or is broken, you may **regain 5
ST**. This ST recovery roll becomes 10/15/20 at 4th/7th/10th Rank,
respectively.

### Unwilling Conduit

2nd Rank Hexblade Hexwrought Curse

When you awaken a spirit of violence while a subject is hexed by you, it
appears within 5 feet of your hexed subject and your hexed subject falls
prone.

# Impulsive Strike

*Strike while the iron is hot.*

At 3rd Rank, you learn the **Impulsive Strike** doomglyph described
below.

### Impulsive Strike

3rd Rank Hexblade Doomglyph

As a reaction at any point, you may increase your doom DC by 5 to make a
weapon attack. Afterwards, you make a doom check.

# Clutches of Death I

*Hanging over the edge of failure, you pull yourself up by the chains
ensnaring your adversary.*

At 3rd Rank, when you burn a Resolve while you have a creature hexed,
you may cause that creature to become restrained. At the
end of each of the affected creature's turns, they may make a
**Fortitude** saving throw against your Hexblade Spell Save DC, ending
the effect on a success.

# Ability Improvement

At 3rd, 6th, and 9th Rank, you gain an Ability Improvement. Each time
you gain an Ability Improvement, distribute three permanent +1 increases
to your ability modifiers. You may increase the same ability modifier
multiple times.

The maximum ability modifier that may be achieved is limited by your
character's Level. The maximum is +5 at Level 3, increases to +6 at
Level 6, and finally becomes +7 at Level 9.

# Exploration

At 4th, 7th, and 10th Rank, you gain an Exploration with Hexblade being
the exploring Class. Each time you gain an Exploration, you gain the
following benefit:

- **Bonus Rank.** You choose any Class other than Hexblade and gain one
  Rank in the chosen Class.

# Hexblade Lure Feature

At 4th Rank, you gain a feature for your Hexblade Lure.

# Hexwrought Curses II

*Indecipherable shadows cling to the corners of their mind, your
persistent presence haunting their thoughts.*

At 5th Rank, select one of the following Hexwrought Curse features from
the list below or one listed at an earlier Rank. During the Retrain
downtime activity, you may redo the selection of all of your chosen
Hexwrought Curses.

### Nightmare Virus

5th Rank Hexblade Hexwrought Curse

When a spirit of violence is awakened, your hexed subject becomes
frightened of that spirit of violence until they are no
longer hexed by you.

### Shaky Footing

5th Rank Hexblade Hexwrought Curse

While a subject is hexed by you, their AC is reduced by one guard step
(their AC is either their Full Guard AC, Half Guard AC, or Base AC).

### Unnerve

5th Rank Hexblade Hexwrought Curse

While a subject is hexed by you, they cannot gain advantage on attack
checks made against you.

# Bloodshield

*Your stubborn refusal to despair in the face of failure hardens into a
fragile armor.*

At 6th Rank, you learn the **Bloodshield** doomglyph described below.

### Bloodshield

6th Rank Hexblade Doomglyph

As a free action immediately after you lose HP, you may increase your
doom DC by 7 to gain your Full Guard AC until the end of your next turn.
Afterwards, you make a doom check.

# Shadow Price

*You've measured the costs and paid the interest. The deferred
catastrophe reignites your fighting spirit.*

At 6th Rank, when you succeed on a doom check, you gain one or more of
the following benefits:

- **Split Fate.** If your doom DC is 7 or higher, you may bestow your
  hex on a target within 60 feet of you as a free action. You may choose
  a condition affecting you and cause your hexed target to be afflicted
  with that condition until they are no longer hexed by you.

- **Reverse Fate.** If your doom DC is 12 or higher, you are cleansed of
  the condition chosen by **Split Fate**.

- **Defy Fate.** If your doom DC is 17 or higher, allies within 15 feet
  of you are also cleansed of the condition chosen by **Split Fate**.

# Lifesteal I

*Sap their life force to recoup the cost of their demise.*

At 7th Rank, twice per long rest, as a free action whenever you roll
your hex die, you may **heal HP** equal to the total of the hex die.
Effects that multiply your hex die, such as critical hits, consequently
improve the healing gained from **Lifesteal**.

# Exploration

8th Rank Hexblade Hexwrought Curse

### Recurse

### Violence Is Mine

10th Rank Hexblade Doomglyph

# Lifesteal II

*Tear their vitality, rend their soul, bring forth from their body your
retribution.*

At 11th Rank, you may now use **Lifesteal** three times per long rest.
Additionally, when you use **Lifesteal**, you may replace the outcome of
the rolled hex dice with their maximum value.

# Hexwrought Curses IV

*Purge every purity and sharpen every hesitation into a weapon designed
for your ends.*

At 11th Rank, select one of the following Hexwrought Curse features from
the list below or one listed at an earlier Rank. During the Retrain
downtime activity, you may redo the selection of all of your chosen
Hexwrought Curses.

### Blindspot

11th Rank Hexblade Hexwrought Curse

When you bestow a hex on a creature, they become **uncertain** of you
and cannot **perceive** you while hexed by you.

### Call to Cull

11th Rank Hexblade Hexwrought Curse

When you awaken a spirit of violence while a subject is hexed by you, it
appears within 5 feet of your hexed subject. As an action during this
turn, you may cause that spirit of violence to make two reaper special
melee attacks against your hexed subject.

### Devour Essence

11th Rank Hexblade Hexwrought Curse

When your hexed subject reaches 0 HP or is broken, the next subject
hexed by you this turn is stunned until the end of the
turn.

# Master of Violence

*Controlling the chaos is the answer, not giving up the power.*

At 12th Rank, when you use your **Violence Is Mine** doomglyph, you may
take two actions and automatically fail your doom check to choose all of
your awakened spirits of violence instead of just one.

# Final Reach

When you reach character Level 12, you compute your *Total Rank*, which
is calculated as:

###### The sum of all of your Class Ranks

If your Total Rank is less than 16 when you reach Level 12, then for
each deficient Rank, you may choose one of the benefits below to modify
a feature granted by one of your Class Ranks:

- **Maneuvers.** You learn two additional maneuvers.

- **Battlecasting.** You gain a maneuver, an additional Spell Point, and
  an additional prepared spell for that Class.

- **Spellcasting.** You gain three additional Spell Points and three
  additional prepared spells for that Class.

# Hexblade Lure: The Abyss

*A sourceless voice calls to dive deeper, to push farther, to blur the
unfathomably thin line between you and the void. It dares you to test
its waters, but at the brink of catastrophe, the bliss of doing the
impossible is the breath of air on the other side of drowning.*

## Adrenaline Spike

*A chilling pang of commitment at the moment where triumph is the only
option.*

At 1st Rank, as a free action when you make a combat or effort check,
you may add 1d4 to the check's total and deal **injuring cold damage**
to yourself equal to 5 × ½ your Tier Bonus. You may repeat this multiple
times for the same check.

## Slick Trick

*The offer is not a price but a challenge eagerly accepted.*

At 1st Rank, you learn the **Slick Trick** doomglyph described below.

### Slick Trick

Lure of the Abyss Hexblade Doomglyph

Immediately before you make a combat or effort check, you raise its DC
by 2. If you succeed on that check, you gain *slick* until the start or
end of combat. Afterwards, regardless of the result, you increase your
doom DC by 4 and make a doom check.

Slick does not stack, and as a free action you may expend slick to
cleanse yourself of any condition other than
incapacitated, stunned, or
frozen.

## Drowned Spirit

*A jealous hand reaches from the nameless void to wrest away your
achievements.*

At 1st Rank, when a spirit of violence hits you with a reaper attack,
you lose slick if you had it.

## Flow State

*Live in the moment and submit to the current.*

At 4th Rank, when you succeed on a combat or effort check, you may
bestow your hex on a target creature within 60 feet of you.

## Fearless Fathoms

*Fear is an illusion created by the mind to prevent greatness.*

At 8th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

- **Conquer Fear.** Expending slick may now be used to cleanse yourself
  or a target creature within your Reach of any condition.

- **Death Defying.** The dice you add to checks for your **Adrenaline
  Spike** feature become d6s.

# Hexblade Lure: Avarice

*The endless torrent of gold passes through your fingertips, tempting
you with the next big play. Whether fancy or fallacy, you are gripped
with the madness of wealth beyond comprehension, taxing your mind with
its idyllic gleam. But with an opportunity this rich, only a fool would
cash out early.*

## Fate's Wager

*The glittering shine of fate's providence is yours to play with. Go big
or go home.*

At 1st Rank, you have a *hoard* of 5 *fortune chips* that resets at the
start and end of combat. The hoard may increase or decrease but has a
maximum of 20 and a minimum of 0.

As a reaction immediately before a target creature within 60 feet of you
attempts a combat check, you may spend any number of fortune chips you
have and bet on either the check's success or on the check's failure:

- **If you're right,** you gain double the number of fortune chips that
  were spent.

- **If you're wrong,** you gain nothing.

- **If the result was an automatic failure or success or was willingly
  chosen to fail,** then you are refunded the chips you spent.

## Gambler Spirit

*Those who lost everything, seeking your company in their misery.*

At 1st Rank, when a spirit of violence hits you with a reaper attack,
you lose 3 fortune chips. When a spirit of violence you awakened is
destroyed, you gain 3 fortune chips.

## Poisoned by Greed

*Money talks, fools listen.*

At 1st Rank, you learn the **Poisoned by Greed** doomglyph described
below.

### Poisoned by Greed

Lure of Avarice Hexblade Doomglyph

As a free action, you spend 3 fortune chips and increase your doom DC by
4 to inflict poisoned on a target creature within 30 feet
of you until either the end of combat or you use this doomglyph again.
Afterwards, you make a doom check.

## Ill-Gotten Gains

*Those who deal with bad luck know the exact price.*

At 4th Rank, when a target creature within 60 feet of you fails a check,
you may spend 3 fortune chips to bestow your hex on them.

## High Roller

*When it has been aces all the way down, you can't stop while you're on
a hot streak.*

At 8th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

- **Dragon's Envy.** Your hoard's initial size becomes 10.

- **Golden Tomb.** When you use **Poisoned by Greed**, you may spend 15
  chips instead of 3. If you do, the poisoned condition is
  replaced with stunned.

# Hexblade Lure: The Unknown

*The incessant pounding of your heart when you first taste new magic
wakes a hunger of the mind that outweighs the caution of the soul. You
push past the lies of mortal knowledge into the unfortunate truths
beyond. Your fractional sliver of endless fractal secrets will never be
complete, but temperance is for the unambitious.*

## Corrupted Knowledge

*Undeniable secrets buried in the forgotten corners of history.*

At 1st Rank, when you prepare one or more Occult spells, you may
*corrupt* any number of them. Corrupted Occult spells do not cost Spell
Points when you cast them as a prepared spell, but you must use the
**Spellburn** doomglyph described below.

### Spellburn

Lure of the Unknown Hexblade Doomglyph

After casting a corrupted spell, but before it begins, you increase your
doom DC by 4 and lose HP equal to your Tier Bonus × the Circle of spell
cast. After the spell begins, you make a doom check.

## Spellcaster Spirit

*An insatiable censor vying to siphon your stolen knowledge.*

At 1st Rank, the reaper attack of spirits of violence you have awakened
is now a special ranged attack against a target within 30 feet of it.

## Spell Locust

*Holding onto a wonderfully horrifying idea for even the briefest moment
changes your life irreparably.*

At 1st Rank, once per rest when you are about to burn a Resolve, you may
instead choose to lose a corrupted spell you have prepared to gain the
effects of burning a Resolve.

## Hexslinger

*A foul and twisted shot of reality from the wastes of infinite
obscurity.*

At 4th Rank, when you hit a target with a cantrip attack, you may bestow
your hex on them.

## Wonderlust

*Devour the forgotten memories and venture into the fringes of the
unknowable.*

At 8th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

- **Feeling Lucky.** When you use **Spellburn**, you may double the
  increase to your doom DC. If you do, halve the HP you lose.

- **Harvest Magic.** When a creature destroys one of your spirits of
  violence, that creature regains 1 expended Spell Point.

- **Hexdraw.** You may use **Hexslinger** to bestow your hex on a
  subject immediately before you target it with a cantrip attack instead
  of when you hit it.
