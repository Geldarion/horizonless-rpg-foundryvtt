**Maximum Stamina (ST) gained per Rank:** 7

# Divine Battlecasting

As a Champion, you are able to prepare and cast spells from the Divine
spell list:

- **Spells Prepared.** At 1st Rank, you may prepare 4 spells for this
  Class at the end of each long rest. You may cast those prepared spells
  using this feature.

- **Class Circle Cap.** Each spell you prepare may have a maximum
  baseline Circle of 1st Circle.

- **Heightening.** When you cast spells using this feature, you may
  heighten spells up to your Class Circle Cap.

- **Spell Points.** At 1st Rank, you gain 2 Spell Points. When you cast
  spells using this feature, you must expend Spell Points equal to the
  Circle of the spell. Spell Points are regained at the end of each long
  rest.

- **Spellcasting Modifier.** Your spellcasting modifier when casting
  spells using this feature is CHA.

  - **Your Spell Attack Bonus** = your CHA + your Tier Bonus

  - **Your Spell Save DC** = 8 + your CHA + your Tier Bonus

  - **Spellcasting Checks** made with spells prepared for this class use
    your CHA.

- **Signature Spell.** Your designated signature spell may be one of the
  1st Circle or higher spells prepared for this feature.

- **Higher Ranks.** At higher Ranks, the spells prepared, Spell Points,
  and Class Circle Cap all improve as described in the Class Overview
  Table.

# Champion Legend

*When the world cries out for aid, silently invoking the Legend's name,
you take the mantle and carry it forward.*

At 1st Rank, you choose a Champion Legend, a timeless echo of heroes
past that you bring to life through your actions. You gain features from
your chosen Champion Legend at 1st, 4th, and 8th Rank.

# Rally I

*An icon rises above the hills. No matter the form of your banner, be it
heraldry or metaphor, the faithful gather underneath and take their
stand.*

At 1st Rank, as an action, you may create your *rally*, which is a 10
foot radius sphere. When you create a rally, you choose for it to be
placed in either of the below manners:

- **Borne.** The rally originates from your location and moves with you
  as you move.

- **Planted.** The rally originates from a target surface within 60 feet
  of you (it does not move). You may not create your rally again unless
  it is within your Reach.

You may also take an action to dismiss your rally, and creating a second
rally dismisses the previous one.

Your chosen Champion Legend grants additional effects to your rally and
provides a list of saving throw types. When an ally makes a listed
saving throw while in your rally, they may add +2 to the total. This +2
bonus is your *rally bonus*, and it becomes +3/+4/+5 at 4th/7th/10th
Rank, respectively.

# Ward

*A bond forged in battle, the security of your presence fills your ally
with the calm of camaraderie.*

At 2nd Rank, as an action or as a free action when you take the Raise
Shield action, you may *place your ward*, causing a target ally within
60 feet of you to become *warded* until the end of combat or until you
place your ward again.

While warded, an ally gains their Half Guard AC. After a warded ally
takes damage from an attack, the ward *splinters*, which also ends the
ward.

Lastly, you may take the Raise Shield action even if your fighting style
does not have the **Shield-Ready** trait and without needing a shield.
If you do, you gain your Half Guard AC from the action regardless of
your STR.

# Ward Reinforcements I

*No matter what, I'm here for you.*

At 2nd Rank, select one Ward Reinforcement feature of your choosing from
the list below, which enhances the capabilities of your **Ward**
feature. You may Retrain this feature to redo the selection of all of
your chosen Ward Reinforcement features.

### Affirmation

2nd Rank Champion Ward Reinforcement

As a reaction when an ally warded by you makes a damage roll, you may
splinter the ward to add **2d6** to the damage roll. This damage roll
bonus becomes 4d6/6d6/8d6 at 4th/7th/10th Rank, respectively.

### Recuperation

2nd Rank Champion Ward Reinforcement

When your wards splinter, you may cause creatures of your choice within
10 feet of the warded ally to **regain 5 ST**. This ST recovery roll
becomes 10/15/20 at 4th/7th/10th Rank, respectively.

### Sharing

2nd Rank Champion Ward Reinforcement

Immediately after you are affected by a spell you cast, you may also
cause an ally warded by you to be affected in the same way by the same
spell effect.

# Collective Force I

*Together we are stronger.*

At 3rd Rank, once per rest, you may place your ward on your initiative
partner as a free action. If you do, you and your initiative partner
both gain *collective force* until the end of the current turn.

When a creature with your collective force hits with an attack, they may
expend it to add **Xd8 force damage** to the damage roll, where X is
your Champion Rank.

# Rally Confidence

*We have so got this.*

At 3rd Rank, whenever you or an ally adds your rally bonus to a saving
throw, they also **regain ST** equal to 3 × your Champion Rank.

# Ability Improvement

At 3rd, 6th, and 9th Rank, you gain an Ability Improvement. Each time
you gain an Ability Improvement, distribute three permanent +1 increases
to your ability modifiers. You may increase the same ability modifier
multiple times.

The maximum ability modifier that may be achieved is limited by your
character's Level. The maximum is +5 at Level 3, increases to +6 at
Level 6, and finally becomes +7 at Level 9.

# Exploration

At 4th, 7th, and 10th Rank, you gain an Exploration with Champion being
the exploring Class. Each time you gain an Exploration, you gain the
following benefit:

- **Bonus Rank.** You choose any Class other than Champion and gain one
  Rank in the chosen Class.

# Champion Legend Feature

At 4th Rank, you gain a feature for your Champion Legend.

# Ward Reinforcements II

*Your allies need you. Every arrow of the enemy you deflect, break, or
take yourself is a victory.*

At 5th Rank, select one of the following Ward Reinforcement features
from the list below or one listed at an earlier Rank. During the Retrain
downtime activity, you may redo the selection of all of your chosen Ward
Reinforcements.

### Clarity

5th Rank Champion Ward Reinforcement

When you place your ward onto an ally, you may cleanse the warded ally
of the disoriented, poisoned, and
frightened conditions.

### Intercession

5th Rank Champion Ward Reinforcement

As an action, you may splinter one of your wards to cause hostile
creatures within 10 feet of the warded ally to become
provoked by you until the start of your next turn.

### Preservation

5th Rank Champion Ward Reinforcement

While an ally is warded by you, they have DB 10 against **supernatural
damage**. This DB becomes 15/20 at 7th/10th Rank, respectively.

# Rally II

*Campfire disagreements fade in battle, as all your friends look to you
for guidance and inspiration.*

At 6th Rank, your rally is improved in the following ways:

- **Radius.** The radius of your rally becomes 15 feet.

- **Saving Throw.** You may choose another type of saving throw and
  append it to the list provided by your Champion Legend. During the
  Retrain downtime activity, you may redo your selection from this
  feature.

The types of saving throws are **Poise**, **Reflex**, **Fortitude**,
**Quick-Wits**, **Will**, and **Courage**.

# Timely Ward

*At the precipice of death, in the moments where it truly matters, your
hand is ready to catch them.*

At 6th Rank, as a reaction at any point, you may place your ward.

# Ability Improvement

At 6th Rank, you gain an Ability Improvement. Each time you gain an
Ability Improvement, distribute three permanent +1 increases to your
ability modifiers. You may increase the same ability modifier multiple
times.

The maximum ability modifier that may be achieved is limited by your
character's Level. The maximum is +5 at Level 3, increases to +6 at
Level 6, and finally becomes +7 at Level 9.

# Final Stand

*To get to them, you must go through me.*

At 7th Rank, once per long rest as an action, you may gain the following
benefits until the start of your next turn:

- **In Harm's Way.** All damage dealt to allies within your rally is
  redirected to you.

- **Endless Resolve.** Burning Resolve does not expend it.

# Exploration

At 7th Rank, you gain an Exploration with Champion being the exploring
Class. Each time you gain an Exploration, you gain the following
benefit:

- **Bonus Rank.** You choose any Class other than Champion and gain one
  Rank in the chosen Class.

# Ward Reinforcements III

*The strength of your bond is a fortress built brick by brick, battered
by the enemy, but cemented with every deed and word of devotion.*

At 8th Rank, select one of the following Ward Reinforcement features
from the list below or one listed at an earlier Rank. During the Retrain
downtime activity, you may redo the selection of all of your chosen Ward
Reinforcements.

### Extrication

8th Rank Champion Ward Reinforcement

When you place your ward onto an ally, they may immediately take the
Dodge action as a free action.

### Immutable

8th Rank Champion Ward Reinforcement

As a free action, you may splinter one of your wards to cleanse the
warded ally of the incapacitated,
polymorphed, stunned, and
unconscious conditions.

### Justice

8th Rank Champion Ward Reinforcement

When one of your wards splinters, you and the warded ally each gain
advantage on your next attack check until the end of your next turn.

# Champion Legend Feature

At 8th Rank, you gain a feature for your Champion Legend.

# Explosive Ward

*The shards of faith punish the faithless.*

At 9th Rank, as a free action, you may splinter one of your wards to
deal **radiant damage** equal to 2 × your CHA to creatures of your
choice within 10 feet of the warded ally.

# Ability Improvement

At 9th Rank, you gain an Ability Improvement. Each time you gain an
Ability Improvement, distribute three permanent +1 increases to your
ability modifiers. You may increase the same ability modifier multiple
times.

The maximum ability modifier that may be achieved is limited by your
character's Level. The maximum is +5 at Level 3, increases to +6 at
Level 6, and finally becomes +7 at Level 9.

# Rally III

*When enemies abound, you carve a path to the heart of their forces and
cut it out.*

At 10th Rank, your rally is improved in the following ways:

- **Radius.** The radius of your rally becomes 20 feet.

- **Relocation.** When your **Planted** rally is within your Reach, you
  may create your rally as a free action. If you create another
  **Planted** rally this way, it must be placed away from you further
  than your Reach.

# Exploration

At 10th Rank, you gain an Exploration with Champion being the exploring
Class. Each time you gain an Exploration, you gain the following
benefit:

- **Bonus Rank.** You choose any Class other than Champion and gain one
  Rank in the chosen Class.

# Collective Force II

*Our glorious fate lies before us, awaiting our action and our belief.
This is the time to strike true.*

At 11th Rank, immediately before a creature with your collective force
makes an attack check, they may expend it to automatically succeed
instead of rolling. Expending collective force in this manner does not
add **Collective Force's** bonus to the damage roll.

# Ward Reinforcements IV

*History may remember what we do, but we remember who we do it for.*

At 11th Rank, select one of the following Ward Reinforcement features
from the list below or one listed at an earlier Rank. During the Retrain
downtime activity, you may redo the selection of all of your chosen Ward
Reinforcements.

### Aegis

11th Rank Champion Ward Reinforcement

When an ally warded by you is attacked, the attacker must make a
**Will** saving throw against your Champion Spell Save DC. On a failure,
the attacker must either change the target of the attack or cause the
attack to miss.

### Salvation

11th Rank Champion Ward Reinforcement

When one of your wards splinters, the warded ally **regains 20 ST**. As
a free action when this occurs, you may expend 2 Spell Points to convert
this ST recovery roll to instead be an HP healing roll.

### Transcendence

11th Rank Champion Ward Reinforcement

When you place your ward onto an ally, they may choose either one
expended usage of a feature that is normally regained on a short rest,
an expended die from a dice pool, or an expended Spell Point, and regain
it. Each ally may gain the benefit of **Transcendence** at most once per
rest.

# Sanctuary of the Resolved

*Looking up towards the gleaming standard in your final moments, you
watch as the purity of your cause burns to ash amidst the cruelty of
your enemies. But a single cinder of hope finds purchase in your heart.
Fight for this, one last time.*

At 12th Rank, once per rest as a free action, when an ally in your rally
is about to burn a Resolve, you may instead dismiss the rally to grant
all allies in your rally the effects of burning a Resolve, except they
do not fall prone.

# Final Reach

When you reach character Level 12, you compute your *Total Rank*, which
is calculated as:

###### The sum of all of your Class Ranks

If your Total Rank is less than 16 when you reach Level 12, then for
each deficient Rank, you may choose one of the benefits below to modify
a feature granted by one of your Class Ranks:

- **Maneuvers.** You learn two additional maneuvers.

- **Battlecasting.** You gain a maneuver, an additional Spell Point, and
  an additional prepared spell for that Class.

- **Spellcasting.** You gain three additional Spell Points and three
  additional prepared spells for that Class.

Champion Legends

# Champion Legend: Confidence

*Sword shaking, the soldier cowers in the face of the endless army.
Whispers from behind ripple towards the soldier as ranks part, a hero
splitting the river of friendly forces that face the unfriendly sea.
Their steady gaze evaluates the enemy and shows no fear; their
deliberate advance is an unvoiced challenge to the forces of evil. The
drive to overcome grows in lockstep with the hero's footfalls, and as
with many great stories in the past, the path to glory is paved with
unflinching bravery.*

## Distracting Challenge

*I'll hold them off!*

At 1st Rank, when you hit with a melee attack against a creature, you
may cause the target to become provoked by you until the
end of combat or after they attack you. While provoked in
this manner, a creature gains advantage on attack checks made against
you.

## Audacious Rally

*Wherever danger lurks, you are soon to follow. A brilliant glimmer
beams from beyond, guiding you to your destiny.*

At 1st Rank, once per turn when you place your rally and choose for it
to be **Planted**, you may add 30 feet to your movement pool for each
hostile creature in the rally. This movement may only be spent traveling
towards your rally.

Lastly, your rally bonus may be applied to saving throw types from the
following list: **Reflex**, **Courage**. Other features can add saving
throw types to this list.

## Warding Drive

*When your foes are on the back foot and are cut down as they threaten
your friends, you are staunch in your ability to protect them.*

At 4th Rank, once per turn when you take the Stagger action, you may
place your ward on a target ally within 60 feet of you as a free action.

## Unbroken

*You are invincible by the fact that nothing has stopped you so far.*

At 8th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

- **All In.** When you place your ward on an ally, they gain advantage
  on the next attack check they make this turn against a creature that
  you have provoked from your **Distracting Challenge**
  feature.

- **Thrill of the Melee.** When you end your turn within your rally, you
  may **regain ST** equal to 4 × the number of hostile creatures within
  your rally.

# Champion Legend: Foresight

*Fruitless debate rages into the late hours of the night, paralyzed by
indecision over impossible outcomes. The din of verbal battle is pierced
by a single drop of distilled wisdom from the hero. Tirelessly
listening, they plot a course through the shifting storm of needs for
all parties. One small choice ignites a guiding star for those who will
follow.*

## Turn of Fate

*In the split second when the tides of battle shift, you see another
path.*

At 1st Rank, as a reaction before a target ally within 60 feet of you
takes damage, you may cause the target to **regain Xd6 ST**, where X is
½ your Tier Bonus.

## Sheltering Rally

*The weary need respite, and you are there to provide. An island of
safety stands tall and protects those within.*

At 1st Rank, creatures of your choice within your rally gain DB 5
against damage dealt by subjects outside of your rally. This DB becomes
10/15/20 at 4th/7th/10th Rank, respectively.

Lastly, your rally bonus may be applied to saving throw types from the
following list: **Fortitude**, **Quick-Wits**. Other features can add
saving throw types to this list.

## Warding Vigilance

*You exist not in the moment but in every moment where you are needed.*

At 4th Rank, once per turn when you take the Anticipate action, you may
place your ward.

## Safe Harbor

*Through expert coordination, rations can turn to plenty. None shall be
denied aid as your ranks swell with the desperate.*

At 8th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

- **No Moment Missed.** You may now use **Turn of Fate** as a reaction
  at any point targeting an ally within 60 feet of you.

- **Rally to Safety.** As a reaction at the end of any turn, you may
  cause a target ally within 60 feet of you to travel up to their Speed
  towards your rally.

# Champion Legend: Tenacity

*The waters rise as homes are torn into distant memories, trapping a
little girl on a shrinking island. When all seems lost, eyes pressed
closed in terror, the silent prayers of her ragged voice are answered by
steady arms, lifting her above the floodwaters. One safely delivered
amongst thousands, the night's work is far from concluded as many more
miracles have yet to be wrought.*

## Tenacious Barrier

*Your stubbornness and empathy interlinks into a formidable
manifestation, shielding your allies when they are most vulnerable.*

At 1st Rank, as an action, you may create a *tenacious barrier*,
creating a 10 foot square originating from a target location within 30
feet of you oriented in any manner of your choosing. Your tenacious
barrier persists until the end of combat or when you create another one.
Your tenacious barrier is a translucent obstruction with 10 HP, 10 AC,
and 10 Toughness. The Toughness of your tenacious barrier becomes
15/20/25 at 4th/7th/10th Rank, respectively.

## Stoic Rally

*A threadbare standard flutters in the harsh winds and biting rain,
defiantly held together by tough seams and faith. It is only over when
you give up.*

At 1st Rank, you and allies in your rally affected by the
charmed, grappled, provoked,
restrained, slowed, or stuck
conditions may treat those conditions as having the **Recoverable**
condition trait, allowing an action to end the effect.

Lastly, your rally bonus may be applied to saving throw types from the
following list: **Poise**, **Will**. Other features can add saving throw
types to this list.

## Warding Recovery

*When they overcome adversity to find their footing, you keep them on
their feet.*

At 4th Rank, once per turn when a condition is removed from an ally
within the range of placing your ward, you may place your ward targeting
that ally as a free action.

## Beacon of Safety

*We can weather anything, as long as we lend a helping hand.*

At 8th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

- **Batten the Hatches.** When you create a tenacious barrier, you may
  have it instead be a 15 foot square originating from a target location
  within 60 feet of you.

- **Bounce Back.** All conditions now count for your **Stoic Rally**
  feature.
