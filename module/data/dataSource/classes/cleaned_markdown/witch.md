# Occult Spellcasting

As a Witch, you are able to prepare and cast spells from the Occult
spell list:

- **Spells Prepared.** At 1st Rank, you may prepare 6 spells for this
  Class at the end of each long rest. You may cast those prepared spells
  using this feature.

- **Class Circle Cap.** Each spell you prepare may have a maximum
  baseline Circle of 1st Circle.

- **Heightening.** When you cast spells using this feature, you may
  heighten spells up to your Class Circle Cap.

- **Spell Points.** At 1st Rank, you gain 4 Spell Points. When you cast
  spells using this feature, you must expend Spell Points equal to the
  Circle of the spell. Spell Points are regained at the end of each long
  rest.

- **Spellcasting Modifier.** Your spellcasting modifier when casting
  spells using this feature is INT.

  - **Your Spell Attack Bonus** = your INT + your Tier Bonus

  - **Your Spell Save DC** = 8 + your INT + your Tier Bonus

  - **Spellcasting Checks** made with spells prepared for this class use
    your INT.

- **Signature Spell.** Your designated signature spell may be one of the
  1st Circle or higher spells prepared for this feature.

- **Higher Ranks.** At higher Ranks, the spells prepared, Spell Points,
  and Class Circle Cap all improve as described in the Class Overview
  Table.

# Witch Coven

*You are part of the Coven, and they are part of you.*

At 1st Rank, you choose a Witch Coven, whose mission and support give
you purpose and power. You gain features from this chosen Witch Coven at
1st, 4th, and 8th Rank.

# Familiar

*Your Coven's ambassador, sent to aid you in whichever form best suits
your needs and the Coven's priorities.*

At 1st Rank, your Coven grants you a *familiar* that has certain
characteristics defined by your Coven. At the end of a rest, you may
*call* your familiar, causing it to appear beside you. Once called, your
familiar has the following properties:

- **Object.** Your familiar is a Tiny object with HP equal to your
  maximum HP, an AC of 15, and 10 Toughness. It has a Speed of 30 feet.
  When destroyed, its physical form disappears and you lose access to
  any of its features until it is called again.

- **Direct.** Once per round during combat as a free action, you may
  have your familiar travel up to its Speed.

- **Focus.** When you cast a cantrip spell, you may forgo the need for a
  spellcasting focus by casting it using your familiar's position for
  the purposes of targeting.

- **Scrying.** As an action, you may perceive through your familiar's
  senses as if you were located at it. As a free action, you may end
  this effect. This effect counts as being the **Scrying** spell trait.

## Transforming Your Familiar 

You gain the following **Quell and Spell** *Witch Transformation*
feature, as well as one granted by your Witch Coven. These features
*transform* your familiar, causing its physical form to disappear and
you to lose access to any of its features until it is called again.

### Quell and Spell

1st Rank Witch Transformation

When you cast an Occult spell using your **Spellcasting** or
**Battlecasting** features, you may transform your familiar and cast the
spell using its position for the purposes of targeting. If you do,
reduce the spell's cost by 1 Spell Point.

# Blight

*A vacancy in search of a boundary, ensorcelling the victim of your
attention with your relentless spellcraft.*

At 2nd Rank, after a creature is affected with one of your spell effects
or you apply a condition to them, you may additionally *blight* that
creature until the end of combat, until you blight a different creature,
or until you burn a Resolve (the creature is aware of the ways your
blight can end).

When you blight a creature, choose an ability modifier. When a blighted
creature makes a combat check using the chosen ability modifier, they
subtract 1 from the total. This penalty becomes 2/3/4 at 4th/7th/10th
Rank, respectively.

# Blight Maladies I

*Where bad luck meets bad blood.*

At 2nd Rank, select one of the following Blight Malady features from the
list below, which enhance the capabilities of your **Blight** feature.
During the Retrain downtime activity, you may redo the selection of all
of your chosen Blight Maladies.

### Doubt

2nd Rank Witch Blight Malady

Your blighted creature's last remaining power requires an additional
action to use.

### Fragility

2nd Rank Witch Blight Malady

When your blighted creature is dealt damage, the damage taken reduction
from their DBs is halved.

### Shame

2nd Rank Witch Blight Malady

Whenever your blighted creature fails a saving throw, you may deal
**1d10 psychic damage** to them. This damage becomes 2d10/3d10/4d10 when
you reach 4th/7th/10th Rank, respectively.

# Seat at the Council

*You sink into your mind and gather the Coven. The many shapes of your
familiar report back their gathered intelligence.*

At 3rd Rank, at the end of each rest, you may ask the GM one question
and name a location. You will receive an answer as if you were currently
present at that location.

# Ability Improvement

At 3rd, 6th, and 9th Rank, you gain an Ability Improvement. Each time
you gain an Ability Improvement, distribute three permanent +1 increases
to your ability modifiers. You may increase the same ability modifier
multiple times.

The maximum ability modifier that may be achieved is limited by your
character's Level. The maximum is +5 at Level 3, increases to +6 at
Level 6, and finally becomes +7 at Level 9.

# Exploration

At 4th, 7th, and 10th Rank, you gain an Exploration with Witch being the
exploring Class. Each time you gain an Exploration, you gain the
following benefit:

- **Bonus Rank.** You choose any Class other than Witch and gain one
  Rank in the chosen Class.

# Witch Coven Feature

At 4th Rank, you gain a feature for your Witch Coven.

# Unrelenting Blight

*What has been fated cannot be shaken by mere willpower.*

At 5th Rank, when your blighted creature burns a Resolve, they remain
blighted.

# Council's Intervention

*The Council knows what plans it has for you and intends for you to
prosper in them.*

At 6th Rank, once per rest when you burn a Resolve, *The Council
intervenes*, granting you one of the following benefits of your choice:

- **Health.** You heal all lost HP.

- **Wealth.** You gain advantage on all checks until the end of your
  next turn.

- **Power.** You regain the free use of your signature spell if you had
  previously expended it.

# Increased Familiarity

*You are now on a "First True Name basis."*

At 6th Rank, once per rest as an action, you may call your familiar to
appear at a target location within 5 feet of you.

# Ability Improvement

At 6th Rank, you gain an Ability Improvement. Each time you gain an
Ability Improvement, distribute three permanent +1 increases to your
ability modifiers. You may increase the same ability modifier multiple
times.

The maximum ability modifier that may be achieved is limited by your
character's Level. The maximum is +5 at Level 3, increases to +6 at
Level 6, and finally becomes +7 at Level 9.

# Exploration

At 7th Rank, you gain an Exploration with Witch being the exploring
Class. Each time you gain an Exploration, you gain the following
benefit:

- **Bonus Rank.** You choose any Class other than Witch and gain one
  Rank in the chosen Class.

# Blight Maladies II

*Once a price, once a vice, and once again, to make up thrice.*

At 8th Rank, select one of the following Blight Malady features from the
list below or one listed at an earlier Rank. During the Retrain downtime
activity, you may redo the selection of all of your chosen Blight
Maladies.

### Binding

8th Rank Witch Blight Malady

Whenever your blighted creature fails a saving throw, you may reduce
their Speed by 10 feet while they are blighted, to a minimum of 5 feet.
This effect can stack on itself.

### Exhaustion

8th Rank Witch Blight Malady

Whenever your blighted creature makes a power recharge check, you may
subtract 1 from the total.

### Ire

8th Rank Witch Blight Malady

Critical hits scored on your blighted creature add all of the dice
rolled in the damage roll an additional time (in the absence of other
features, dice are tripled rather than doubled).

# Witch Coven Feature

At 8th Rank, you gain a feature for your Witch Coven.

# Council's Warning

*Amidst an ever-growing threat, there is no time to wait for the
consequences.*

At 9th Rank, at the beginning of combat, The Council intervenes.

# Ability Improvement

At 9th Rank, you gain an Ability Improvement. Each time you gain an
Ability Improvement, distribute three permanent +1 increases to your
ability modifiers. You may increase the same ability modifier multiple
times.

The maximum ability modifier that may be achieved is limited by your
character's Level. The maximum is +5 at Level 3, increases to +6 at
Level 6, and finally becomes +7 at Level 9.

# Bloodbound Familiar

*When the opportunity arises, your familiar won't hesitate to inhabit a
greater form.*

At 10th Rank, as a reaction when a creature of Tier I or greater within
60 feet of you dies or is knocked out, you may designate it as a
*vessel* and call your familiar. Your familiar appears at the vessel's
location, and it uses the vessel's Speed, mobilities, and Size instead
of its own. When you transform your familiar, or if the vessel becomes
no longer knocked out or dead, the vessel is returned to its original
state in the location where the familiar transformed.

# Exploration

At 10th Rank, you gain an Exploration with Witch being the exploring
Class. Each time you gain an Exploration, you gain the following
benefit:

- **Bonus Rank.** You choose any Class other than Witch and gain one
  Rank in the chosen Class.

# Blight Eyes

*Give them the evil eye.*

At 11th Rank, once during each of your turns as a free action, you may
blight a target creature within 30 feet of you.

# Welcome to the Coven

*As the hand of the Coven, you have labored to earn great power from the
many generations past. In hours of dire need, such formalities can be
waived for those you trust with our greatest purpose.*

At 12th Rank, whenever The Council intervenes, you may have **Health**,
**Wealth**, and **Power** all occur. Each benefit may be applied to
yourself or a target ally, but a creature may only be chosen once per
intervention.

# Final Reach

When you reach character Level 12, you compute your *Total Rank*, which
is calculated as:

###### The sum of all of your Class Ranks

If your Total Rank is less than 16 when you reach Level 12, then for
each deficient Rank, you may choose one of the benefits below to modify
a feature granted by one of your Class Ranks:

- **Maneuvers.** You learn two additional maneuvers.

- **Battlecasting.** You gain a maneuver, an additional Spell Point, and
  an additional prepared spell for that Class.

- **Spellcasting.** You gain three additional Spell Points and three
  additional prepared spells for that Class.

Witch Covens

# Witch Coven: Alterity

*Fields scorched, disease running unfettered, and kingdoms brought to
waste - these are the implements your Coven uses to enact its vengeful
metamorphosis. Ever hungering and digging through the world's muddied
cruelty, you are tasked with unearthing the rot and manifesting its
truest nature. Ruin is a mercy compared to letting it fester.*

### Familiar Properties

Your familiar has basic Burrowing mobility. Examples of forms it could
take include rats, worms, dogs, crabs, or spoons.

## Infectious Notion

*Your ideas catch flame with undeniable power.*

At 1st Rank, you may append **Smoldering Crown** to the list of spells
you prepare for the Witch class. Some spells have a *contagion* trait as
indicated in the spell's description. In addition to the creature
affected by a spell's contagion trait, you may also cause an additional
target creature within that spell's range to become affected by the
spell as if it had failed the saving throw (in absence of other
features, this causes the spell's effects to spread to two targets
rather than one).

## Gnawing Infection

*A withering decay claws ever deeper into their soul.*

At 1st Rank, you gain the **Gnawing Infection** Witch Transformation
feature described below.

### Gnawing Infection

Coven of Alterity Witch Transformation

When you create a spell effect that targets one or more creatures and
requires concentration, you may transform your familiar to grant
**Gnawing Infection** to the spell effect. At the end of each of your
turns, you may deal **1d8 poison damage** to all creatures affected by
your spell effects that have **Gnawing Infection** . This damage becomes
2d8/3d8/4d8 at 4th/7th/10th Rank, respectively.

## Rotted Roots

*They look past the eyes and into a soul wretching under the unfaltering
curses. Perhaps it is too late.*

At 4th Rank, all creatures you have affected with spell effects with the
contagion trait are provoked toward your blighted
creature.

## Eldritch Omen

*Insidious ruin made manifest in myriad forms: allegiances crumble to
dust, disease tightens its grip, and unabated fire purges the earth.*

At 8th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

- **Ring of Fire.** When creatures fail the saving throw imposed by your
  **Smoldering Crown** spell, they become ablaze.

- **Sow Dissent.** When a creature becomes affected by a contagion spell
  and is provoked by **Rotted Roots**, they must take
  their next action to make a weapon attack against the blighted
  creature if possible.

# Witch Coven: Life

*To give, you must also take. Fundamental truths of the balance of life
forces permeate the theory of the magic that draws vigor from your foes
to bolster your allies. Tipping the scale still comes at great cost.*

### Familiar Properties

Your familiar has basic Flying mobility. Examples of forms it could take
include birds, bats, dragonflies, or flapping books.

## Exuberant Offering

*The collection and administration of this gift is up to the user's
discretion.*

At 1st Rank, you gain the **Exuberant Offering** Witch Transformation
feature described below.

### Exuberant Offering

Coven of Life Witch Transformation

As an action, you may transform your familiar to gain three *vital
charges*. At the start and end of combat, all vital charges are
discarded.

## Channel Vitality

*A surgical thumb on the scale of death.*

At 1st Rank, you may append **Sanguine Sliver** to the list of spells
you prepare for the Witch class.

When you cast a spell of 1st Circle or higher that deals immediate
damage to a single target, you may expend vital charges equal to the
spell's Circle to cause a different target creature within range to
**regain ST** equal to the half of the amount of initial damage taken by
the target of this spell.

## Subtle Tithe

*Cheating the rules of magic comes at a price.*

At 4th Rank, when you expend one or more vital charges to cause a
creature to regain ST, you may instead deal half that amount as
**necrotic damage** to both your blighted creature and yourself.
Additionally, once per round when you blight a creature, you may gain
one vital charge.

## Stream of Vigor

*You express your mastery of the ebb and flow of life itself within your
sanguine symphony.*

At 8th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

- **Bloody Bargain.** When you use **Exuberant Offering**, you gain five
  vital charges instead of three.

- **Cut a Deal.** Using **Channel Vitality** on **Sanguine Sliver** only
  costs one vital charge, regardless of Circle.

# Witch Coven: Lore

*Horrors of the night are feared by most, but you study them. Each
weakness, each defense mechanism, all secrets to be deciphered for the
work of the Coven. The forms with the most novelty are kept close in the
quiet, slithering, creeping things, and the morphologies that stray too
far into the aberrant are swiftly pruned.*

### Familiar Properties

Your familiar has basic Climbing mobility. Examples of forms it could
take include geckos, spiders, cats, snakes, or a lump of clay.

## Studied Dissection

*It begins with stray footnotes in esoteric grimoires and ends with the
carcasses of the monsters who sought to thwart you.*

At 1st Rank, you may add the **Researcher** specialization to your
Adventuring Trade (this does not count towards the number of
specializations on that trade).

Once per turn, immediately after you make an Analyze check against a
creature, you may use any feature you have to cast a cantrip that makes
a spell attack against the same creature (if they are within the spell
effect's range). The spell's casting time is shortened to a free action,
and if you used one or more knowledge bombs on the Analyze check, you
may activate its **Discharge** trait as a free action.

## Transmutation Ring

*Powerful sigils inscribed in a circle of malevolent punishment of
maladaptation.*

At 1st Rank, you gain the **Transmutation Ring** Witch Transformation
feature described below.

### Transmutation Ring

Coven of Lore Witch Transformation

As an action, you may transform your familiar and designate a 5 foot
radius circle originating at the floor directly beneath your familiar
called a *transmutation ring* that persists until the end of combat.
When you create a transmutation ring, you choose an **elemental**,
**biological**, or **supernatural** damage type. Whenever a creature
casts a spell while on a transmutation ring, all damage dealt by any of
the spell's effects will be converted into the transmutation ring's
chosen damage type.

## Cryptic Key

*Unlock the tender information hidden inside.*

At 4th Rank, you gain a +2 bonus on all Research checks. Additionally,
spell attack checks made against your blighted creature have their
critical hit threshold reduced by 2.

## Cull The Weak

*You step in when natural ecological pressures are not up to the task.*

At 8th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

- **Crack the Case.** When you use **Studied Dissection**, if you use
  two knowledge bombs on the Analyze check, and you hit with the
  cantrip's spell attack, you may double all dice in the damage roll.

- **Unnatural Selection.** Immediately after you make an Analyze check,
  you may change one of your transmutation ring's chosen damage types to
  a different **elemental**, **biological**, or **supernatural** damage
  type.
