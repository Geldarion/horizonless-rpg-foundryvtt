# Harmonic Spellcasting

As a Maven, you are able to prepare and cast spells from the Harmonic
spell list:

- **Spells Prepared.** At 1st Rank, you may prepare 6 spells for this
  Class at the end of each long rest. You may cast those prepared spells
  using this feature.

- **Class Circle Cap.** Each spell you prepare may have a maximum
  baseline Circle of 1st Circle.

- **Heightening.** When you cast spells using this feature, you may
  heighten spells up to your Class Circle Cap.

- **Spell Points.** At 1st Rank, you gain 4 Spell Points. When you cast
  spells using this feature, you must expend Spell Points equal to the
  Circle of the spell. Spell Points are regained at the end of each long
  rest.

- **Spellcasting Modifier.** Your spellcasting modifier when casting
  spells using this feature is CHA.

  - **Your Spell Attack Bonus** = your CHA + your Tier Bonus

  - **Your Spell Save DC** = 8 + your CHA + your Tier Bonus

  - **Spellcasting Checks** made with spells prepared for this class use
    your CHA.

- **Signature Spell.** Your designated signature spell may be one of the
  1st Circle or higher spells prepared for this feature.

- **Higher Ranks.** At higher Ranks, the spells prepared, Spell Points,
  and Class Circle Cap all improve as described in the Class Overview
  Table.

# Maven Realm

*Great power can be drawn from a unique perspective.*

At 1st Rank, you choose a Maven Realm that describes how your presence
bends the rules of the universe and fashions a new fate for you and your
friends. You gain features from your chosen Maven Realm at 1st, 4th, and
8th Rank.

# Sphere of Influence I

*Sinking into the fabric of reality, the weight of your presence pulls
those nearby into your influence, the gravitas of your gravity creating
a sharp discontinuity in the world around you. Near you, anything could
be possible.*

At 1st Rank, as a free action after you cast a spell, you may create
your *sphere of influence*, which is a 20 foot radius sphere originating
from yourself. When you travel, the sphere of influence's origin point
may travel with you. Your sphere of influence persists until you dismiss
the effect as a free action at any point or if you are no longer in the
sphere.

Creatures within your sphere of influence are subject to the following
effects:

- **Boundary Conditions.** If a creature other than you travels or
  teleports out of the sphere of influence, that creature becomes
  disoriented until the end of the current turn.

- **Realm.** Creatures in your sphere of influence are affected by your
  Maven Realm features.

# Inspiration I

*Your presence lifts up those in need with every word of encouragement.*

At 2nd Rank, you have a pool of 2d6 called your *inspiration dice pool*.
As a reaction, when a target creature other than you within your sphere
of influence makes a saving throw, you may *inspire* them, allowing them
to add one inspiration die to the total.

Various features allow you to inspire creatures when they make other
kinds of checks or impact rolls. Regardless of how they are used,
inspiration dice are removed from your inspiration dice pool when they
are rolled. At the end of every rest, your inspiration dice pool is
refilled.

# Inspiration Ideals I

*Many great works begin with a simple idea.*

At 2nd Rank, select one of the following Inspiration Ideal features from
the list below, which enhance the capabilities of your **Inspiration**
feature. During the Retrain downtime activity, you may redo the
selection of all of your chosen Inspiration Ideals.

### Boldness

2nd Rank Maven Inspiration Ideal

Whenever you inspire a creature, you may cause them to **regain ST**
equal to 2 × your Maven Rank.

### Flow

2nd Rank Maven Inspiration Ideal

When you inspire a creature making a check and they still fail, the
rolled inspiration dice are not removed from your inspiration dice pool.

### Mastery

2nd Rank Maven Inspiration Ideal

As a reaction, when a target creature other than you within your sphere
of influence makes an attack or combat check, you may inspire them,
allowing them to add one inspiration die to the total.

# Prodigious Talent

*When the time comes, you deliver your passions to the furthest extent
of perfection.*

At 3rd Rank, once per rest, when you make a trade check that is possible
for you to succeed, you may choose to succeed it regardless of the
total. **Prodigious Talent** may not be used on checks without a DC,
such as a Craft check.

# Ability Improvement

At 3rd, 6th, and 9th Rank, you gain an Ability Improvement. Each time
you gain an Ability Improvement, distribute three permanent +1 increases
to your ability modifiers. You may increase the same ability modifier
multiple times.

The maximum ability modifier that may be achieved is limited by your
character's Level. The maximum is +5 at Level 3, increases to +6 at
Level 6, and finally becomes +7 at Level 9.

# Exploration

At 4th, 7th, and 10th Rank, you gain an Exploration with Maven being the
exploring Class. Each time you gain an Exploration, you gain the
following benefit:

- **Bonus Rank.** You choose any Class other than Maven and gain one
  Rank in the chosen Class.

# Maven Realm Feature

At 4th Rank, you gain a feature for your Maven Realm.

# Inspiration Ideals II

*The layered and infectious thematics bring the world to a fever pitch.*

At 8th Rank, select one of the following Inspiration Ideal features from
the list below or one listed at an earlier Rank. During the Retrain
downtime activity, you may redo the selection of all of your chosen
Inspiration Ideals.

### Contrast

8th Rank Maven Inspiration Ideal

When you inspire a creature making a check with any degree of
disadvantage, they may replace the outcomes of any inspiration dice
rolled with their maximum value.

### Intensity

8th Rank Maven Inspiration Ideal

When you inspire a creature making a check, you may allow them to add a
second inspiration die to the total.

### Presence

8th Rank Maven Inspiration Ideal

You may expend 1 Spell Point, choose one of your features that inspires
a creature as a reaction, and instead use it as a free action.

At 9th Rank, you gain an Ability Improvement. Each time you gain an

# Sphere of Influence III

*Your magical prowess is diffuse through the air, sparking an electric
notion of the furthest corners of your universe falling within striking
distance. The rules have changed once more.*

At 10th Rank, when you create your sphere of influence, you may increase
the sphere's radius up to 60 feet in total. Additionally, you may modify
spell effects you create if they fit either of the following criteria:

- **Area.** If the spell effect designates an area, you may treat
  creatures of your choosing in your sphere of influence as being within
  the area.

- **Multiple Targets.** If the spell effect can target two or more
  creatures, you may additionally target any number of creatures of your
  choosing in your sphere of influence.

# Multiversal Talent

*Your vision is filled with the many lives and decisions your allies
could have made, yet they are here with you now. Knowing their many
permutations and alternate paths, you grant them a glimpse of one more
suited for the task at hand.*

At 11th Rank, you may use **Prodigious Talent** on any effort check any
ally within 60 feet of you makes if it is possible for them to succeed.

# Harmonious Instant

*The greatest elegance is achieved when all motions of the universe
coincide to a single moment in time. Within that handful of seconds,
anything is possible.*

At 12th Rank, once per rest, as a free action during your turn, you may
grant yourself the following benefits until the start of your next turn:

- **Aligned Perfectly.** When any of your inspiration dice is rolled and
  its outcome is even, that die is not removed from your inspiration
  dice pool.

- **At the Right Place.** Once during your turn, you may allow yourself
  and creatures of your choosing in your sphere of influence to teleport
  to an unoccupied space that they can target within your sphere of
  influence.

- **At the Right Time.** Your features that inspire creatures as a
  reaction may now instead be used as a free action.

# Final Reach

When you reach character Level 12, you compute your *Total Rank*, which
is calculated as:

###### The sum of all of your Class Ranks

If your Total Rank is less than 16 when you reach Level 12, then for
each deficient Rank, you may choose one of the benefits below to modify
a feature granted by one of your Class Ranks:

- **Maneuvers.** You learn two additional maneuvers.

- **Battlecasting.** You gain a maneuver, an additional Spell Point, and
  an additional prepared spell for that Class.

- **Spellcasting.** You gain three additional Spell Points and three
  additional prepared spells for that Class.

Subclasses

# Maven Realm: Dreams

*In the space betwixt the squinting of the eye lies fertile ground for
your imaginationary magic. Your boundless wonder suffuses the air with a
dreamy perfume masking the tug and pull drawing ever deeper into
slumber. The answer to the outside is only found within.*

## Blended Boundary

*Your powers thrive in the lack of focus - an eternal gradient to slip
further and further away.*

At 1st Rank, you may append **Drowsiness** to the list of spells you
prepare for the Maven class. Additionally, any effect you create with
the **Out of It** condition trait (such as the **Drowsiness** spell)
does not end for a creature when they take damage if that damage did not
reduce their HP.

## Painted Dreamscape

*There is no life without a vision for how it must be. The architecture
of your ambition melts and drips and leaks into the hardened,
crystalline reality.*

At 1st Rank, as an action while your sphere of influence is active, you
may designate a target creature that you have affected with the **Out of
It** condition trait and *dreamspin* them. The target is forcibly
teleported to a target unoccupied space within your sphere of influence
and becomes *dreamspun* while you have them affected with the **Out of
It** condition trait. You may not dreamspin yourself or a creature you
have dreamspun already.

Dreamspun creatures have the following effects:

- **Mostly Fictitious.** Only you or other dreamspun creatures may be
  affected by them. All damage they deal cannot reduce HP.

- **Within the Dream.** They ignore the **Locked**, **Immobile**, and
  **Inattentive** condition traits (such as by being
  incapacitated or unconscious) but cannot
  move out of your sphere of influence or perceive anything outside of
  your sphere of influence. If you move your sphere of influence such
  that a dreamspun creature is now outside the area, they cease being
  dreamspun.

- **Snap Out of It.** At the end of each of their turns, they may make a
  **Quick-Wits** saving throw against your Maven Spell Save DC. On a
  success, they cease being dreamspun and are cleansed of all effects
  that have the **Out of It** condition trait.

- **Return.** After they cease to be dreamspun, they cannot be dreamspun
  again until they complete a rest.

## Larger Than Life

*Your allies finally see themselves the way you see them.*

At 4th Rank, when you inspire a target creature you have dreamspun, none
of the inspiration dice they roll are removed from your inspiration dice
pool.

## Vibrant Palette

*Striking hues and bold silhouettes string together a masterwork of a
future. The inherent strength of an idea lies only in its presentation.*

At 8th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

- **Dreamrunner.** Creatures may choose to ignore the **Return** effect
  of being dreamspun.

- **Heavy Daze.** You may choose for a creature to become
  stuck while affected by your **Drowsiness** spell
  effects.

- **Twinspin.** As a free action, when you dreamspin a creature, you may
  dreamspin a second qualifying creature.

# Maven Realm: Possibility

*Storms crackle, fortunes twist, and nations fall as the penpoint of
fate meets with the page of history. With only a moment to spare, the
cautious author directs the poetic procession - but not all mistakes
must be taken as fact. A choice word at the exact moment can ease
tempests, portend victories, and forge alliances.*

## Rough Draft

*A story can take many turns and a skilled writer knows to survey the
landscape before putting it to words.*

At 1st Rank, once per round when a creature becomes affected by one of
your spell effects, you may additionally give all affected creatures
other than yourself a *rewrite* that persists until the start of your
next turn. Creatures with one of your rewrites can expend it on the
following:

- **Poetry.** After the creature casts a spell and knows the results of
  all of its immediate effects, they may expend a rewrite to negate all
  of the spell's effects and regain all actions and reactions taken to
  cast it. If they do, they may instead cast a different spell using any
  feature.

- **Prose.** After a creature makes an attack and misses, they may
  expend a rewrite to retry the attack check against a different target.
  This counts as the same attack for the purposes of weapon attack
  fatigue and maneuver repetition penalties.

## Dramatic Retelling

*A larger-than-life story can be a biography if you live large enough.*

At 1st Rank, all dice rolled for impact rolls by creatures in your
sphere of influence become d12s. For example, if a creature in your
sphere of influence would roll 1d8 + 1d4 + 6 for a damage roll, they
instead roll 2d12 + 6.

## Editorial Hand

*Impossibility is for the unimaginative.*

At 4th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

- **Eloquence.** When a target creature within your sphere of influence
  expends a rewrite for **Poetry**, you may inspire them, allowing them
  to add one of your inspiration dice to the spell effect's Spell DC.

- **Emphasis.** When a target creature within your sphere of influence
  expends a rewrite for **Prose**, you may inspire them, allowing them
  to add one of your inspiration dice to the retried attack check's
  total. Inspiration dice added to this check become d12s.

## Patchwork Narrative

*Incidental moments given retroactive meaning coincide to create layered
plotlines and foreshadowed tension, all neatly tied together just in the
nick of time.*

At 8th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

- **Plot Hole.** When a creature expends a rewrite for **Prose**, they
  may teleport to an unoccupied space they target within half their
  Speed before retrying the attack.

- **Turn of Phrase.** When a creature expends a rewrite for **Poetry**,
  they refund any feature usages or Spell Points expended by casting the
  negated spell.

# Maven Realm: Resonance

*A stirring melody sweeps through the crowd and pulls their heartbeats
into tune. The winds, seas, and stars sing their verses of the song of
creation and harmonize with the key of your masterpiece. A complete
chorus permeates space as you rise and uplift.*

## Aural Attunement

*Singing a song of desire for a better world, for dreams unfulfilled,
the hero takes center stage.*

At 1st Rank, as an action, you may designate a target creature within 30
feet of you with your *aural attunement*, which persists until you can
no longer target the creature or you take this action again. Once per
round, when you cast a 1st Circle or higher spell, you may cause the
creature with your aural attunement to **regain ST** equal to 5 × the
spell's Circle.

## Resonant Tempo

*No baton in hand, but with wandless magic nonetheless, you conduct the
conflict like a battle of rhythm and melody.*

At 1st Rank, when you create your sphere of influence, you may choose
either **Adagio** or **Allegro** to be in effect while it persists:

- **Adagio.** All d20 outcomes of attack and combat checks made by
  creatures in your sphere of influence that are higher than 10 become
  10.

- **Allegro.** All d20 outcomes of attack and combat checks made by
  creatures in your sphere of influence that are lower than 10 become
  10.

## Spherical Harmony

*A piercing melody broadened into harmonious leitmotif, the action of
one is played out by the chorus.*

At 4th Rank, as a reaction, when a target creature in your sphere of
influence is dealt damage, you may expend an inspiration die to inspire
them and cause the damage dealt to be redirected to be evenly divided
amongst all creatures in the sphere of influence.

## Moving Reverberance

*The power of a single cadence can bring hope to the heart or
devastation to the psyche.*

At 8th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

- **Anthem.** When you inspire a creature, you may switch **Resonant
  Tempo's** active effect from **Adagio** to **Allegro**.

- **Ballad.** When the creature with your aural attunement breaks their
  ST, you may switch **Resonant Tempo's** active effect from **Allegro**
  to **Adagio**.
