**Maximum Hit Points (HP) gained per Rank:** 9

**Maximum Stamina (ST) gained per Rank:** 9

# Maneuvers

At 1st Rank, you learn three maneuvers, and you learn an additional one
each time you gain an additional Fighter Rank. You may Retrain this
feature to redo your selection of known maneuvers.

# Fighter Code

*Confidence and purpose are your truest whetstones.*

At 1st Rank, you choose a Fighter Code to exemplify your combat style
and techniques. You gain features from your chosen Fighter Code at 1st,
4th, and 8th Rank.

# Momentum I

*Every movement and attack plan carries forth into the next in seamless
choreography. Keep looking forward and never repeat yourself.*

At 1st Rank, when you perform a maneuver with a listed repetition
penalty, but don't incur it, you may *exhaust* the maneuver. Doing so
immediately grants one *momentum die*, which are d12s (these are added
to your *momentum dice pool*). Afterwards, performing the exhausted
maneuver always incurs its listed repetition penalty.

At the start and end of combat, you discard all momentum dice from your
momentum dice pool and remove exhaustion from all of your maneuvers.

Lastly, you know the **Decisive Blow** feature described below.

### Decisive Blow

Once per turn, as a free action immediately before you make a weapon
attack, you may expend one momentum die to grant the following benefits:

- **Bonus Damage.** If the attack hits, you may add 1d12 to the damage
  roll.

- **Stagger.** If the attack scores a critical hit or its attack check's
  total exceeds the target's AC by 5 or more, the target becomes
  staggered.

- **Fighter Code.** Your chosen Fighter Code may add an additional
  effect.

# Counter Vigil

*Your muscles tense as you study your foe with disciplined vigilance,
awaiting the moment they create an opening.*

At 2nd Rank, at the beginning of each round of combat, you may choose to
*watch* a target hostile creature. If you do, you are unable to take
your turn until the creature you are watching completes their turn.
**Counter Vigil** cannot be used on a creature using a similar feature
that prevents them from taking their turn until another creature does.

When you take your turn after watching a creature, you cease watching
the creature and the turn is called your *rebuttal*, which grants the
following effects:

- **Momentum Die.** You gain one momentum die.

- **Perfect Opportunity.** The creature you watched becomes
  staggered.

If the creature you are watching dies or is knocked out, you may choose
to watch a different target creature.

# Vigilant Tactics I

*A steady focus underlies the greatest offense.*

At 2nd Rank, select one of the following Vigilant Tactic features from
the list below, which enhance the capabilities of your **Counter Vigil**
feature. During the Retrain downtime activity, you may redo the
selection of all of your chosen Vigilant Tactics.

### Battle Flow

2nd Rank Fighter Vigilant Tactic

While you are watching a creature, you gain advantage on **Poise** and
**Reflex** saving throws.

### Keen Eye

2nd Rank Fighter Vigilant Tactic

When you begin watching a creature, you may choose to make a Search
check or an Analyze check as a free action.

### Second Wind

2nd Rank Fighter Vigilant Tactic

At the start of your rebuttal, you may **regain ST** equal to 2 × your
Fighter Rank.

# Double Strike I

*As their grip on the conflict loosens, two quick strikes deliver a
swift victory.*

At 3rd Rank, once per rest, when you make a weapon attack against a
staggered target, you may make a second weapon attack
against the same target as a free action. This second attack treats the
target as being staggered and ignores weapon attack
fatigue.

# Stalwart I

*A triage of motion and strategy lessens the losses.*

At 3rd Rank, when you make a saving throw, you may expend one momentum
die to add 1d12 to the total.

# Ability Improvement

At 3rd, 6th, and 9th Rank, you gain an Ability Improvement. Each time
you gain an Ability Improvement, distribute three permanent +1 increases
to your ability modifiers. You may increase the same ability modifier
multiple times.

The maximum ability modifier that may be achieved is limited by your
character's Level. The maximum is +5 at Level 3, increases to +6 at
Level 6, and finally becomes +7 at Level 9.

# Exploration

At 4th, 7th, and 10th Rank, you gain an Exploration with Fighter being
the exploring Class. Each time you gain an Exploration, you gain the
following benefit:

- **Bonus Rank.** You choose any Class other than Fighter and gain one
  Rank in the chosen Class.

# Fighter Code Feature

At 4th Rank, you gain a feature for your Fighter Code.

# Momentum II

*An exacting strike balances necessity with assurance.*

At 5th Rank, when you exhaust a maneuver, you gain two momentum dice
instead of one. Additionally, when you apply **Decisive Blow** to a
weapon attack and it hits, you may expend one or more additional
momentum dice and add Xd12 to the damage roll, where X is the additional
momentum dice spent.

For example, if you expend a momentum dice to use **Decisive Blow** and
the attack hit, you could then expend two more momentum dice and add a
total of 3d12 to the damage roll.

# Vigilant Tactics II

*Deep breaths fan the flames of your roaring reprisal.*

At 5th Rank, select one of the following Vigilant Tactic features from
the list below or one listed at an earlier Rank. During the Retrain
downtime activity, you may redo the selection of all of your chosen
Vigilant Tactics.

### Conclusive Strikes

5th Rank Fighter Vigilant Tactic

During your rebuttal, all weapon attacks you make against the creature
you watched while they are staggered have their critical
hit threshold reduced by 1.

### Prescient Attack

5th Rank Fighter Vigilant Tactic

On the first weapon attack you make during your rebuttal against the
creature you watched, the attack may be made at any range provided the
creature was within your fighting style's attack range at some point
while you were watching them.

This attack's critical hit threshold is reduced by 1.

### Rebound

5th Rank Fighter Vigilant Tactic

At the start of your rebuttal as a free action, you may cleanse yourself
of all conditions and effects with the **Recoverable** condition trait,
such as by being prone or ablaze.

# Double Strike II

*Your constant drive pushes to reach further, strike quicker, and hit
harder.*

At 6th Rank, you may now use **Double Strike** twice per rest.
Additionally, each time you use **Double Strike**, you gain one momentum
die.

# Maneuver Mastery I

*Battles begin and end with the fundamentals.*

At 6th Rank, choose two maneuvers that you know and for each chosen
maneuver, choose one of its listed advancements. When you perform the
chosen maneuvers, you may apply its chosen advancement without needing
to meet its ability modifier requirement.

When you take the Retrain downtime activity, you may redo your selection
of maneuvers and their advancements.

# Ability Improvement

At 6th Rank, you gain an Ability Improvement. Each time you gain an
Ability Improvement, distribute three permanent +1 increases to your
ability modifiers. You may increase the same ability modifier multiple
times.

The maximum ability modifier that may be achieved is limited by your
character's Level. The maximum is +5 at Level 3, increases to +6 at
Level 6, and finally becomes +7 at Level 9.

# Unflinching

*I could go all day.*

At 7th Rank, on your turn as free action, you may expend a momentum die
to gain the following benefits:

- **Dogged.** If you are affected by the **Immobile** condition trait
  (which sets your Speed to 0 and prevents you from traveling), you may
  replace it with being affected by the **Slow** condition trait (which
  doubles the movement cost to travel).

- **Persistent.** If you are affected by the **Locked** condition trait
  (which normally prevents you from taking actions), you may take one of
  your turn's actions regardless.

# Exploration

At 7th Rank, you gain an Exploration with Fighter being the exploring
Class. Each time you gain an Exploration, you gain the following
benefit:

- **Bonus Rank.** You choose any Class other than Fighter and gain one
  Rank in the chosen Class.

# Vigilant Tactics III

*A trained eye can catch every mistake and move to capitalize on them*

At 8th Rank, select one of the following Vigilant Tactic features from
the list below or one listed at an earlier Rank. During the Retrain
downtime activity, you may redo the selection of all of your chosen
Vigilant Tactics.

### Guarded Stance

8th Rank Fighter Vigilant Tactic

While you are watching a creature, you gain your Half Guard AC.

### Coordination

8th Rank Fighter Vigilant Tactic

While you are watching a creature, attacks made by your allies against
that creature have their critical hit threshold reduced by 1.

### Spring to Action

8th Rank Fighter Vigilant Tactic

At the start of your rebuttal, you may add your Speed to your movement
pool.

# Fighter Code Feature

At 8th Rank, you gain a feature for your Fighter Code.

# Momentum III

*Every movement brings the heat in measures untold.*

At 9th Rank, when you exhaust a maneuver, you gain three momentum dice
instead of two. Additionally, when you use **Double Strike**, you may
remove exhaustion from one of your maneuvers.

# Maneuver Mastery II

*Every minute perfection in your routine is another weapon in your
arsenal.*

At 9th Rank, you may choose four maneuvers instead of two for your
**Maneuver Mastery** feature (you still choose one advancement for each
maneuver).

# Ability Improvement

At 9th Rank, you gain an Ability Improvement. Each time you gain an
Ability Improvement, distribute three permanent +1 increases to your
ability modifiers. You may increase the same ability modifier multiple
times.

The maximum ability modifier that may be achieved is limited by your
character's Level. The maximum is +5 at Level 3, increases to +6 at
Level 6, and finally becomes +7 at Level 9.

# Stalwart II

*You stand defiant in the face of utter annihilation.*

At 10th Rank, when you use **Stalwart**, you may expend one additional
momentum die to add another 1d12 to the saving throw's total (adding a
maximum total of 2d12 altogether). Additionally, when you use
**Stalwart** on a saving throw and it still fails, you regain all of the
expended momentum dice.

# Exploration

At 10th Rank, you gain an Exploration with Fighter being the exploring
Class. Each time you gain an Exploration, you gain the following
benefit:

- **Bonus Rank.** You choose any Class other than Fighter and gain one
  Rank in the chosen Class.

# Momentum IV

*You are there when it truly counts, each and every time.*

At 11th Rank, when you exhaust a maneuver, you gain four momentum dice
instead of three. Additionally, when you burn a Resolve, you may remove
exhaustion from all of your maneuvers.

# Vigilant Tactics IV

*Your opponents shall fear the quiet just as much as the storm it
foretells.*

At 11th Rank, select one of the following Vigilant Tactic features from
the list below or one listed at an earlier Rank. During the Retrain
downtime activity, you may redo the selection of all of your chosen
Vigilant Tactics.

### Measured Strike

11th Rank Fighter Vigilant Tactic

Once during each of your rebuttals, when you make a weapon attack
against the creature you watched, you may add a +10 bonus to the attack
check's total.

### Relentless

11th Rank Fighter Vigilant Tactic

As a reaction, when the watched creature burns a Resolve, either while
you are watching them or during your rebuttal, you may make a weapon
attack against that creature.

### Rushdown

11th Rank Fighter Vigilant Tactic

At the start of your rebuttal, you may allow your initiative partner to
travel up to their Speed towards the creature you watched.

# Flawless Execution

*Your training, effort, and mastery culminates in an ultimate display of
prowess sure to topple the greatest of enemies and sever their claim to
power.*

At 12th Rank, as two actions, you may make two weapon attacks against
each staggered target within the range of your weapon
attacks. All of these attacks automatically hit, but cannot score
critical hits (as such, you do not need to roll any of the attack
checks). Features that apply effects to these weapon attacks if their
attack total meets a certain threshold, such as your **Decisive Blow**
feature, act as if the total is sufficiently high enough.

# Final Reach

When you reach character Level 12, you compute your *Total Rank*, which
is calculated as:

###### The sum of all of your Class Ranks

If your Total Rank is less than 16 when you reach Level 12, then for
each deficient Rank, you may choose one of the benefits below to modify
a feature granted by one of your Class Ranks:

- **Maneuvers.** You learn two additional maneuvers.

- **Battlecasting.** You gain a maneuver, an additional Spell Point, and
  an additional prepared spell for that Class.

- **Spellcasting.** You gain three additional Spell Points and three
  additional prepared spells for that Class.

Subclasses

# Fighter Code: Commander

*The gift of a quiet combat is only given to those who know that
planning is where the battle truly lies. You do not chase the amateur's
dream of a singular, decisive move to sweep the battlefield. Instead,
the perfected strategy of small choices overwhelms the strongest
defense. For those who know their allies and their enemies best, also
know the path to victory.*

## Encircle

*Fighting with a few allies is no different than fighting with an army
under your command; it all comes down to effective cues and signals.*

At 1st Rank, as an action once per turn, you may cause a target creature
within 30 feet of you to make a **Quick-Wits** saving throw against your
Maneuver DC. On a failure, the target becomes *encircled* until the end
of the current turn. An encircled creature's AC is reduced to their Base
AC and it cannot be improved while encircled.

## Flanking Blow

*Forming a single front against an enemy split into fractions, the whole
is pitted against separate parts of a whole.*

At 1st Rank, as an action, you may allow your initiative partner to take
a reaction to make a light weapon attack. You may apply **Decisive
Blow** to that attack instead of one of your own weapon attacks this
turn.

## Direct the Troops

*The onset of soldiers is like the rush of a river; even the stones will
roll in its path.*

At 4th Rank, while you are watching a creature, they are encircled.

## Pressure Defenses

*Should the enemy strengthen their vanguard, they will weaken their
rear. Should they strengthen their left flank, they weaken their right.
You direct maximum force to minimum resistance.*

At 8th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

- **Leader's Reputation.** While you have a creature encircled, they are
  also frightened of you.

- **Unexpected Flank.** You may use **Flanking Blow** on any target ally
  instead of only your initiative partner.

# Fighter Code: Drifter

*Roads winding under the sun and dreams wandered under the stars mark
the paths of those with somewhere to go. As a skilled nomad, you never
let your enemies know your footing, darting through the fields as fallen
foes lie in your wake. A life spent in motion has trained you to press
ever onward.*

## Passing Strike

*You dash past an enemy and leave behind a mortal wound.*

At 1st Rank, once on your turn after you take the Move action but before
your movement pool is empty, you may make a light weapon attack against
a target you have not yet attacked this turn. Afterwards, until the end
of the current turn, you may not make any additional attacks against the
attack's target and must spend the remainder of your movement pool.

## Gliding Blow

*Dust kicks up into the wind as you leap into the fray.*

At 1st Rank, when you apply **Decisive Blow** to a weapon attack, you
may immediately travel up to 10 feet either before or after the attack.

## Shift

*Even as you study the battle before you, you remain mobile to keep the
upper hand.*

At 4th Rank, while you are watching a creature, you may travel up to 10
feet each time your combat team takes initiative. You do not gain
**Shift's** benefit on your rebuttal.

## Windswept Stride

*You outrun the confines of your own weight and sprint with defiant
fluidity as you cut your way through their ranks.*

At 8th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

- If you take two or more Move actions during your turn, you may use
  **Passing Strike** twice on your turn instead of once.

- When you use **Gliding Blow**, you may travel up to 20 feet instead of
  10 feet.

# Fighter Code: Vanguard

*A soldier's final moments are spent watching their nation fall. With
the necessity of fortitude hammered into memory, you stand tall and
protect the vulnerable.*

### Vanguard Range

By default, your *vanguard range* is equal to your Reach. If you are
using a melee fighting style, this distance may instead be your weapon
attack range.

## Hold the Line

*Pressing forward and anchoring at the front, you keep the enemy at
bay.*

At 1st Rank, while you have your Half Guard or Full Guard AC, the
movement cost is doubled for hostile creatures traveling through 5 foot
spaces within your vanguard range.

## Guarded Blow

*A strong offense must not sacrifice one's vigilance.*

At 1st Rank, when you apply **Decisive Blow** to a weapon attack,
regardless if it hits or misses, you may gain your Half Guard AC until
the start of your next turn.

## Disruption

*Your grip on the battlefront is unshakable.*

At 4th Rank, once per turn, while you are watching a creature within
your vanguard range and they make an attack against a subject other than
you, you may impose disadvantage on the attack check as a free action
(this must be done before the attack check is rolled).

## Iron Bastion

*You become the wall that breaks the tide.*

At 8th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

- **Locked in Battle.** When you use **Guarded Blow** and the weapon
  attack hits, the target becomes stuck until the end of
  the current turn. This effect has the **Recoverable** condition trait,
  allowing an action to end it early.

- **Towering Presence.** While you have your Full Guard AC, your
  vanguard range is increased by 10 feet.
