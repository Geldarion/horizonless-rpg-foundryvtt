# **List of Alchemist Items**

## **Bombs**

## **\[C\] Fire Bomb**

**Rarity:**				Common

**Discipline:**				Alchemist

**Type:**					Consumable (Bomb)

**Crafting Threshold Score:** 	30

## *Light them up.*

	As a Use Item action, you may expend this item to make a ranged special attack using your DEX against a target within 30 feet of you. On a hit, you deal **2d6 fire damage** to the target. Alternatively, you may instead make a Wreck an Object check against the target and add \+2 to the total.

Regardless if the check succeeds or fails, you also designate a 10 foot radius sphere originating from the target. The target and all creatures and objects in the area are dealt an additional **1d8 fire damage**.
