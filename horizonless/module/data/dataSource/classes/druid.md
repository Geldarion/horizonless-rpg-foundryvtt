**Maximum Hit Points (HP) gained per Rank:** 5

**Maximum Stamina (ST) gained per Rank:** 5

# **Primal Spellcasting**

	As a Druid, you are able to prepare and cast spells from the Primal spell list:

* **Spells Prepared.** At 1st Rank, you may prepare 6 spells for this Class at the end of each long rest. You may cast those prepared spells using this feature.

* **Class Circle Cap.** Each spell you prepare may have a maximum baseline Circle of 1st Circle.

* **Heightening.** When you cast spells using this feature, you may heighten spells up to your Class Circle Cap.

* **Spell Points.** At 1st Rank, you gain 4 Spell Points. When you cast spells using this feature, you must expend Spell Points equal to the Circle of the spell. Spell Points are regained at the end of each long rest.

* **Spellcasting Modifier.** Your spellcasting modifier when casting spells using this feature is WIS.

  * **Your Spell Attack Bonus** \= your WIS \+ your Tier Bonus

  * **Your Spell Save DC** \= 8 \+ your WIS \+ your Tier Bonus

  * **Spellcasting Checks** made with spells prepared for this class use your WIS.

* **Signature Spell.** Your designated signature spell may be one of the 1st Circle or higher spells prepared for this feature.

* **Higher Ranks.** At higher Ranks, the spells prepared, Spell Points, and Class Circle Cap all improve as described in the Class Overview Table.

# **Druid Path**

	*Etchings of unreadable edicts carved by tremor, wind, and water. Your spoken word is carried not by reverberations of sound but by the erosions of yesteryear and the rebirth of what has yet to pass.*

	At 1st Rank, you choose a Druid Path to embark upon, guiding your way and marking the nature of your power. You gain features from your chosen Druid Path at 1st, 4th, and 8th Rank.

# **Landwords**

	*You sense the veins of the earth swell with an eagerness to change.*

	At 1st Rank, as an action, you may use a *landword* that you know and produce its effect within its *landword area* designated as a 10 foot square originating on a target surface within range. Starting at 1st Rank, you know the **Roiling Earth** landword described below and an additional one from your chosen Druid Path.

	Additionally, after you cast a spell that affects or designates an area upon casting, you may use a landword that you know as a free action. If you use a landword in this manner, the landword is *spellshaped* by the spell and its area becomes the spell's initial affected or designated area.

	Whichever way you use your landwords, you may only use one of them once per round. The effect of a landword is indefinite unless otherwise noted in the effect's description. If a landword is spellshaped, its effect is independent of the spell effect.

	The **Roiling Earth** landword is described below:

### *Roiling Earth*

## *1st Rank Druid Landword*

	This landword's area becomes difficult terrain.

# **Seeds I**

	*In time it will grow. The future forms in the hands of those who inhabit it.*

	At 2nd Rank, at the end of a short or long rest, you may create a *seed*, which is a Tiny wearable item with a Toughness of 20\. When a creature wearing of your seeds is subjected to one or more of the following conditions, they may cause one of their seeds to *bloom*, expending it and immediately cleansing themselves of all of these conditions: ablaze, charmed, disoriented, grappled, pacified, prone, provoked, poisoned, slowed, and stuck.

	At the end of every long rest, all seeds you have previously created are destroyed before you create new seeds.

# **Critter Form**

	*A second pair of eyes to survey every corner of the land. The needs and wants of all shall be known to you.*

	At 2nd Rank, as an action once per rest, you may become polymorphed into a Tier  beast creature of your choosing until the end of your next short or long rest. While polymorphed in this manner, you ignore the **No Concentration** effect listed in the **Transformed** condition trait, allowing you to concentrate on spell effects (though you may not cast spells).

As an action, you may end this effect early.

# **Reformation**

	*You are who moves the earth, reshaping the world to better hold those who rest upon it.*

	At 3rd Rank, you learn the **Rapid Ridge** and **Yawning Cavern** landwords described below:

### *Rapid Ridge*

## *3rd Rank Druid Landword*

	You create a 10 foot high, 5 foot thick wall formed along a straight line whose endpoints are target floors within the landword's area. Each 5 foot section of the wall has HP equal to 10 - your Druid Rank, 10 AC, and 5 Toughness.

### *Yawning Cavern*

## *3rd Rank Druid Landword*

	All surfaces in the landword's area recess up to 10 feet deep (i.e. floors sink into pits and walls form holes). Only material and obstructions of Toughness 5 or below may be destroyed by this landword (dirt has a Toughness of 5 while wood or stone has a Toughness of 10).

# **Ability Improvement**

	At 3rd, 6th, 9th, and 12th Rank, you gain an Ability Improvement. Each time you gain an Ability Improvement, distribute three permanent \+1 increases to your ability modifiers. You may increase the same ability modifier multiple times.

The maximum ability modifier that may be achieved is limited by your character's Level. The maximum is \+5 at Level 3, increases to \+6 at Level 6, and finally becomes \+7 at Level 9\.

# **Exploration**

	At 4th, 7th, and 10th Rank, you gain an Exploration with Druid being the exploring Class. Each time you gain an Exploration, you gain the following benefit:

* **Bonus Rank.** You choose any Class other than Druid and gain one Rank in the chosen Class.

# **Druid Path Feature**

	At 4th Rank, you gain a feature for your Druid Path.

# **Seeds II**

	*A small token that yearns for the challenges of tomorrow. In all manners it wishes to grow.*

	At 5th Rank, you may add the following conditions to the list that can activate and be cleansed by your seeds blooming: frightened, incapacitated, polymorphed, restrained, and unconscious.

# **Amplified Landwords I**

	*You look upon a world bound into fixation by the hands of mortalkind. This too shall pass.*

	At 6th Rank, once per rest, when you use one of the following landwords, you may enhance its effect as described below:

* **Roiling Earth.** All creatures standing in the landword's area of **Roiling Earth** fall prone.

* **Rapid Ridge.** The Toughness of the wall you create using **Rapid Ridge** becomes 15\. Creatures that climb the wall treat it as difficult terrain.

* **Yawning Cavern.** The maximum Toughness of material you can destroy with **Yawning Cavern** becomes 15\.

# **Dire Form**

	*The journey ahead is far and winding. Channeling a primordial might, you become that which bridges the gap.*

	At 6th Rank, you may use **Critter Form** twice per rest. When you use **Critter Form**, you may cause the creature form you become polymorphed into to gain the following benefits:

* **Size.** You choose the creature form's Size up to Huge.

* **Speed.** The creature form's Speed is increased by 20 feet.

* **Health.** The HP and ST you gain from the **Health** effect of the **Transformed** condition trait both become 30\.

* **Overland Travel.** When traveling over long distances outside of combat, you are able to travel twice as quickly.

# **Ability Improvement**

	At 6th Rank, you gain an Ability Improvement. Each time you gain an Ability Improvement, distribute three permanent \+1 increases to your ability modifiers. You may increase the same ability modifier multiple times.

The maximum ability modifier that may be achieved is limited by your character's Level. The maximum is \+5 at Level 3, increases to \+6 at Level 6, and finally becomes \+7 at Level 9\.

# **Exploration**

	At 7th Rank, you gain an Exploration with Druid being the exploring Class. Each time you gain an Exploration, you gain the following benefit:

* **Bonus Rank.** You choose any Class other than Druid and gain one Rank in the chosen Class.

# **Sacred Grove**

	*A dewdrop of your soul enriches the land and forms a home enwreathed by the wilds.*

	At 8th Rank, choose a location that's important to you, which may be anywhere in the world or even on a different plane. You designate a 50 foot radius, 50 foot tall cylinder centered on that location as your *grove*. Your grove has the following properties:

* **Seclusion.** Dense foliage, earth, or solid material surrounds the sides of your grove.

* **Admittance.** You are notified whenever a creature attempts to enter into your grove, either by teleporting or physically moving into it, and you may choose to bar them from entering.

* **Formation.** You choose how the interior of the grove appears and what is inside of it. Even when significant changes occur outside the grove, the grove itself remains intact.

* **Safety.** Hazardous exterior conditions surrounding the grove do not pose threat to creatures in the grove. For example, if the grove is in the ocean, creatures are able to breathe underwater while in the grove.

* **Seeds.** Whenever you complete a long rest in your grove or take the Arbor downtime activity (described below), you may create a seed for each creature resting with you.

	You may take the *Arbor* downtime activity to create an *arbor* that forms a pathway to your grove that opens at the end of the downtime. Arbors leading into your grove may be any distance away from your grove and even on a different plane of existence. An arbor has two sides: one is in the location where you initially created it, and the other is permanently added to your grove, allowing you to travel back to the other side at any subsequent time.

Your grove and all arbors connected to it are indestructible to all but the 7th Circle **Destroy** spell. If your grove is destroyed, you may take the Arbor downtime activity to designate a new one and create an arbor leading to it.

# **Druid Path Feature**

	At 8th Rank, you gain a feature for your Druid Path.

# **Seeds III**

	*The force of life germinates within the seed, forming pearlescent branches encircling the outer shell. A great power made available in times of great need.*

	At 9th Rank, you may add the following conditions to the list that can activate and be cleansed by your seeds blooming: incapacitated, frozen, and stunned.

# **Ability Improvement**

	At 9th Rank, you gain an Ability Improvement. Each time you gain an Ability Improvement, distribute three permanent \+1 increases to your ability modifiers. You may increase the same ability modifier multiple times.

The maximum ability modifier that may be achieved is limited by your character's Level. The maximum is \+5 at Level 3, increases to \+6 at Level 6, and finally becomes \+7 at Level 9\.

# **Amplified Landwords II**

	*The ground shivers and quakes as the final hours pierce the horizon.*

	At 10th Rank, when you use your **Amplified Landwords** feature, you may use a second landword that you know on the same area as the first (including landwords learned from your chosen Druid Path or paragon feats). This second landword may gain the benefits from your **Amplified Landwords** feature.

# **Exploration**

	At 10th Rank, you gain an Exploration with Druid being the exploring Class. Each time you gain an Exploration, you gain the following benefit:

* **Bonus Rank.** You choose any Class other than Druid and gain one Rank in the chosen Class.

# **Majestic Form**

	*A form suffused with the wonders of magic and the fondness of home. With a graceful leap, you bound through hidden paths into your grove.*

	At 11th Rank, while you have polymorphed yourself, you may gain the following benefits:

* **Spellcasting.** You ignore the **No Spellcasting** and **No Concentration** effects of the **Transformed** condition trait, allowing you to cast spells and concentrate on spell effects.
* **Teleportation.** As an action, you may teleport into an unoccupied space in your grove. If you do, you may take action within the next 10 minutes to teleport back to the nearest unoccupied space of where you came from. For either of these teleportations, creatures you are carrying are teleported with you.

# **Blossoming Earth**

	*Something new can truly begin.*

	At 12th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

* **Amplified Landwords.** You may use your **Amplified Landwords** feature an unlimited number of times per rest.

* **Bountiful Bloom.** As a free action once per rest, you may cause one of your seeds to bloom and cause the wearer to either **regain all of their ST** or **heal all of their HP**.

# **Ability Improvement**

	At 12th Rank, you gain an Ability Improvement. Each time you gain an Ability Improvement, distribute three permanent \+1 increases to your ability modifiers. You may increase the same ability modifier multiple times.

The maximum ability modifier that may be achieved is limited by your character's Level. The maximum is \+5 at Level 3, increases to \+6 at Level 6, and finally becomes \+7 at Level 9\.

# **Final Reach**

	When you reach character Level 12, you compute your *Total Rank*, which is calculated as:

###### The sum of all of your Class Ranks

	If your Total Rank is less than 16 when you reach Level 12, then for each deficient Rank, you may choose one of the benefits below to modify a feature granted by one of your Class Ranks:

* **Maneuvers.** You learn two additional maneuvers.

* **Battlecasting.** You gain a maneuver, an additional Spell Point, and an additional prepared spell for that Class.

* **Spellcasting.** You gain three additional Spell Points and three additional prepared spells for that Class.

# **Druid Path: Flora**

*Amidst the freezing rains the barren cliffs fall away and crumble into the depths below. Within this crushing weight, the roots of the sprawling greenery tighten their grip and brace the earth against cruel desolation. As you beckon the wilds to tame the land, you become the gardener of a brittle world.*

## **Steward's Hand**

	*Bristling vines tear through the ground and throw your opponents.*

	At 1st Rank, you may append the **Bristle Lash** cantrip to the list of spells you have prepared for the Druid class. When you pull a subject using **Bristle Lash**, you may choose to instead produce either of the effects below:

* **Direct.** You instead push the target up to 10 feet in a direction of your choosing.

* **Bind.** You instead cause the target to become stuck with the **Recoverable** condition trait, allowing an action to end the effect.

## **Twisting Brush**

	*An unforeseen beauty emerges from the tangled wildflowers sprouting from the earth.*

	At 1st Rank, you learn the **Twisting Brush** landword described below.

### *Twisting Brush*

## *Flora Druid Landword*

	This landword's area becomes difficult terrain for all creatures hostile to you. Your allies gain basic Climbing mobility and advantage on Vanish checks while in the area.

## **Wrathbriar Seed**

	*The seed blooms with overwhelming vigor and entangles your foe.*

	At 4th Rank, when one of your seeds blooms, you may cause a target within 10 feet of the wearer to make a **Poise** saving throw against your Druid Spell Save DC. On a failure, the target is restrained. Once per round, a creature restrained in this manner may take an action to retry the saving throw, ending the effect on a success.

## **Gnarled Brush**

	*A wave of overgrowth sweeps over the world and guards it against a dire end.*

	At 8th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

* When you cast **Bristle Lash**, you may increase the spell effect's range by 30 feet.

* When you use your **Twisting Brush** landword, you may cause any number of target creatures or objects in the area to make a **Poise** saving throw against your Druid Spell Save DC. On a failure, the target is restrained. Once per round, a creature restrained in this manner may take an action to retry the saving throw, ending the effect on a success.

# **Druid Path: Storms**

*Howling winds herald the approaching storms. Sparks of potential hum at your approach with anticipation of what is to follow. At your hand, you guide the destined retribution and with your voice, you echo the rhythm of justice.*

## **Resounding Static**

	*The rolling thunder continues where the lightning leaves behind.*

	At 1st Rank, whenever you cause a creature to take **lightning damage**, you apply *static* to that creature. As an action once per rest, you may cause all creatures within 60 feet of you that you have applied static to in this combat to make a **Fortitude** saving throw against your Druid Spell Save DC. On a failure, a creature either takes one fewer action on their next turn or takes one fewer action when they burn a Resolve, whichever happens first.

## **Gathering Storm**

	*Electric fractals carve the earth and pave the way.*

	At 1st Rank, you learn the **Gathering Storm** landword described below.

### *Gathering Storm*

## *Storms Druid Landword*

	At the start of your next turn, all creatures and objects in this landword's area are dealt **2d4 lightning damage**. At 4th/7th/10th Rank, this damage roll becomes 4d4/6d4/8d4, respectively.

## **Stormpetal Seed**

	*The bud unfurls with explosive vengeance and enwreathes the bearer with bolts of lightning eager to strike back.*

	At 4th Rank, when one of your seeds blooms, the wearer gains advantage on the next attack they make before the end of combat. If that attack hits, it deals an additional **2d8 lightning damage** (this applies your static to the target).

## **Crackling Thunder**

	*The stormclouds gather and pour their incessant wrath. Those that threaten the ways of the world will meet their opposing force.*

	At 8th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

* When a creature fails the saving throw from your **Resounding Static** feature, you may cause the creature to become incapacitated until the end of its next turn.

* When you use your **Gathering Storm** landword, you may have the damage occur twice: once upon using the landword and once again at the start of your next turn.

# **Druid Path: Waves**

*Your breath carries the brine of the sea and your eyes peer through the waves, beholding the countless wonders beneath. A lifeline to the world, its benevolence reaches its farthest corners and spans all boundaries. As its defender, you protect the waters and welcome those lost in the currents.*

## **Gift of the Sea**

	*With a graceful deluge, you awaken the strength in your allies.*

	At 1st Rank, you are able to breathe underwater. Additionally, you may append **Tidal Blessing** to the list of spells you prepare for the Druid class. Your **Tidal Blessing** spell gains the following improvements:

* **Splash.** When you cast **Tidal Blessing**, you may create a *splash* by designating a 10 foot radius sphere originating from a target of the spell. Creatures of your choosing in the splash (including the target) may **regain 5 ST**. If you create a splash, landwords may be spellshaped by the spell.

* **Cleansing.** All creatures affected by your **Tidal Blessing** spell are also cleansed of all effects that have either the **Recoverable** or **Out of It** condition traits, such as by being prone or unconscious.

## **Surf Turf**

	*Amidst rippling currents, you draw the path forward.*

	At 1st Rank, you learn the **Surf Turf** landword described below.

### *Surf Turf*

## *Waves Druid Landword*

	You may allow one creature of your choosing in this landword's area to take a reaction to travel up to half their Speed. Creatures using this movement may not travel outside this landword's area.

If this landword is spellshaped by a spell, creatures that take this reaction are also immune to any damage or conditions inflicted by the spell effect and automatically succeed on saving throws imposed by it.

## **Oceanboon Seeds**

	*A seed reminiscent of a glistening pearl, shining forth the beauty of the sea and projecting a warding bubble around its bearer.*

	At 4th Rank, creatures that wear your seeds gain the ability to breathe underwater. When one of your seeds blooms, the wearer retains this benefit until the end of their next long rest and may cause all creatures and objects within 10 feet of them to be pushed 5 feet away from the wearer.

## **Benevolent Tides**

	*Crashing waves eddy through your spells and crest with sparkling brilliance. At your behest, the waters guide and embolden the weary.*

	At 8th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

* You may allow any number of creatures in the area of your **Surf Turf** to take a reaction to gains its benefits.

* The ST recovery of your splash for **Tidal Blessing** becomes 10\.

* Creatures affected by your **Tidal Blessing** spell are also cleansed of the incapacitated condition.
