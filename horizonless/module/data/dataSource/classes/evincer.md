**Maximum Hit Points (HP) gained per Rank:** 7

# **Harmonic Battlecasting**

	As an Evincer, you are able to prepare and cast spells from the Evincer spell list:

* **Spells Prepared.** At 1st Rank, you may prepare 4 spells for this Class at the end of each long rest. You may cast those prepared spells using this feature.

* **Class Circle Cap.** Each spell you prepare may have a maximum baseline Circle of 1st Circle.

* **Heightening.** When you cast spells using this feature, you may heighten spells up to your Class Circle Cap.

* **Spell Points.** At 1st Rank, you gain 2 Spell Points. When you cast spells using this feature, you must expend Spell Points equal to the Circle of the spell. Spell Points are regained at the end of each long rest.

* **Spellcasting Modifier.** Your spellcasting modifier when casting spells using this feature is CHA.

  * **Your Spell Attack Bonus** \= your CHA \+ your Tier Bonus

  * **Your Spell Save DC** \= 8 \+ your CHA \+ your Tier Bonus

  * **Spellcasting Checks** made with spells prepared for this class use your CHA.

* **Signature Spell.** Your designated signature spell may be one of the 1st Circle or higher spells prepared for this feature.

* **Higher Ranks.** At higher Ranks, the spells prepared, Spell Points, and Class Circle Cap all improve as described in the Class Overview Table.

# **Evincer Lens**

*You peek behind the curtain with a cracked eye, giving form to synesthetic insight. Raindrops of light trickle in with a photoelectric giddiness as you unwind the truths of the universe.*

	At 1st Rank, you choose an Evincer Lens to focus your power and filter your perception of the universe. You gain features from your chosen Evincer Lens at 1st, 4th, and 8th Rank.

# **Lightspeed**

	*A sliver of aberrant light reaches through the cacophony of the many worlds, falling within your grasp and scattered as paint in a tempest. The boundaries of time and space lose their ground in the presence of a novel power.*

	At 1st Rank, once per round, if your Speed is greater than 0, you may take an action to teleport to a target unoccupied space within a distance equal to your Speed.

Additionally, each time you cause you or any other creature to teleport in any way (such as your **Lightspeed** and **Vergence** features, or any spell that allows you to teleport), you gain a *lightspeed charge* for each creature teleported that you can use to activate *lightforms.* At the start and end of combat, all lightspeed charges are lost.

## **Lightforms Known**

	At 1st Rank, you know the **Flashbang** lightform described below, as well as one from your Evincer Lens.

### *Flashbang*

## *1st Rank Evincer Lightform*

Once per round when you hit with a weapon attack, you may expend a lightspeed charge to convert all of the damage to **radiant** and deal an additional **Xd4 damage**, where X is your Evincer Rank. If this damage breaks the target's ST, they are disoriented until the end of their next turn.

# **Vergence I**

	*The infinitude of reality folds upon itself in uncountable dimensionality and symmetry. Through a flash of mere intuition, consequence itself can join and bifurcate.*

	At 2nd Rank, twice per rest, as a reaction when a target ally within 30 feet is dealt damage, you may teleport to the nearest unoccupied space from them of your choosing and then produce the following effects:

* **Convergence.** You **regain 2 ST** for each ally within 15 feet of you. This amount per ally increases as you gain Evincer Ranks, as shown in the Class Overview Table.

* **Divergence.** You redirect half of the damage to be dealt to you instead.

	Your effects that occur immediately after you teleport, such as **Convergence** and **Divergence**, are *vergence effects* and may be produced in any order you choose.

# **Final Reach**

	When you reach character Level 12, you compute your *Total Rank*, which is calculated as:

###### The sum of all of your Class Ranks

	If your Total Rank is less than 16 when you reach Level 12, then for each deficient Rank, you may choose one of the benefits below to modify a feature granted by one of your Class Ranks:

* **Maneuvers.** You learn two additional maneuvers.

* **Battlecasting.** You gain a maneuver, an additional Spell Point, and an additional prepared spell for that Class.

* **Spellcasting.** You gain three additional Spell Points and three additional prepared spells for that Class.

# **Evincer Lens: Breakneck**

	*The wind rushing by your ears becomes a steady hum, the horizon moves closer to you, and space seems to envelop you as you push the boundaries of movement. From your perspective, it seems less like you're moving fast and more like other things are moving more slowly, the distances between objects shrinking to make traversal trivial. To others, you might as well be in two places at once.*

## **Supersonic**

	*Arcs of errant lightning ripple through your body as you begin to bolt. Discarded energy wisps in your wake while you outrun the consequences.*

	At 1st Rank, once per rest as a free action, you may become *supersonic*. While you are supersonic, each time you take a Move action, you gain one lightspeed charge.

	At the end of each of your turns while supersonic, if you have not used the **Accelerate** lightform (listed below) during that turn, you cease being supersonic and *burn out*. Burning out causes you to fall prone and deal **2d6 lightning damage** to all creatures and objects other than yourself within 10 feet of you**.** This damage increases to 4d6/6d6/8d6 at 4th/7th/10th Rank, respectively.

	At the end of combat, you may cease being supersonic without burning out.

## **Accelerate**

*You push everything from your mind, disregard the physical toll, and will yourself to run.*

	At 1st Rank, you learn the **Accelerate** lightform described below:

### *Accelerate*

## *Breakneck Evincer Lightform*

As a free action while you are supersonic, you may expend a lightspeed charge to increase your Speed by 10 feet until you are no longer supersonic. This effect may stack onto itself up to five times. Each use of this lightform increases its lightspeed charge cost by 1 until you are no longer supersonic.

## **Twister Trail**

	*Zig-zagging through the battlefield, stirring up whirlwinds along the way.*

	At 4th Rank, when you use **Vergence** while supersonic, you may choose not to teleport and instead travel up to your Speed if at the end of this movement you are within 5 feet of the target ally. At the end of your movement, vergence effects still trigger as if you had teleported.

Each creature of your choice within your Reach of the path you traveled during this movement is pushed 10 feet upwards and becomes stuck in the air until the end of their next turn, at which point they fall prone.

## **Slipstream**

	*Finding purchase on even the frictionless grooves of space itself.*

	At 8th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

* **Hyperspeed.** Your **Accelerate** feature increases your Speed by 15 feet rather than 10 feet.

* **Supersonic Boom.** When you burn out, instead of falling prone you may travel 10 feet immediately before dealing the burn out damage. Creatures that take the burn out damage fall prone.

* **Tornado Alley.** Creatures stuck by **Twister Trail** are instead restrained.

# **Evincer Lens: Fractureforged**

	*Glimpses of the true nature of the universe come to many in the form of slivers of light, ripples in water, and patterns of sand. Pulling from the pattern, you extract shards of raw potential from imperceptible corners of the empty space around you. These seemingly colorless prisms need just the right angle of light to produce the most brilliant hues.*

## **Fractal Armament**

	*You crack the edge of reality into cosmic needles.*

	At 1st Rank, as a free action, you may conjure a *fractal armament* which is a weapon purposed for any melee fighting style that is immediately held by either you or an ally within 15 feet of you. It has the following properties:

* **Fragile.** Weapon attacks that hit using the fractal armament cause it to be destroyed afterwards. Otherwise, it lasts until the end of your next turn or it is unattended.

* **Glass Spear.** As an action, a creature holding the fractal armament may make a ranged special attack using your CHA against a target within 30 feet of them, after which the weapon is destroyed. On a hit, the target is dealt **2d8 piercing damage**. This damage becomes 4d8/6d8/8d8 at 4th/7th/10th Rank, respectively.

## **Stained Glass**

	*The fault lines fuse as you align the crystal's form to produce a single, tuned wavelength.*

	At 1st Rank, you learn the **Stained Glass** lightform described below:

### *Stained Glass*

## *Fractureforged Evincer Lightform*

As an action, you may expend a lightspeed charge you may grant a *color* chosen from below to one of your fractal armaments that does not yet have a color:

* **Red.** Target is dealt an additional **3d4 fire damage** and is set ablaze.

* **Orange.** Target is dealt an additional **2d4 force damage** and is stuck until the end of their next turn.

* **Yellow.** Target is dealt an additional **1d4 psychic damage** and is frightened until the end of their next turn.

* **Green.** Target is dealt an additional **2d4 acid damage** and is poisoned until the end of their next turn.

	Weapon attacks that hit using your fractal armaments and **Glass Spear** hits apply the used fractal armament's color's effect to the target.

## **In Case of Emergency**

	*When your glass facade is struck, a brilliant backlight illuminates through the cracks.*

	At 4th Rank, as a vergence effect, you may perform your **Stained Glass** lightform as a free action. Additionally, you gain a new color option for your **Stained Glass** feature:

* **Blue.** Choosing this option costs one additional lightspeed charge. Target is dealt an additional **3d4 cold damage** and is restrained until the end of their next turn.

## **Iridescent Arsenal**

	*Whistling snaps crackle through the air as weapon after weapon is pulled from the shattered veil.*

	At 8th Rank, you gain the following benefits:

* **Kaleidoscope.** You may use **Stained Glass** on two fractal armaments with one action, spending the necessary charges for both.

* **Prism.** You gain a new color option for your **Stained Glass** feature:

  * **Violet.** Choosing this option costs one additional lightspeed charge. Target is dealt an additional **3d4 necrotic damage** and is incapacitated until the end of their next turn.

# **Evincer Lens: Timetorn**

*Embracing the severed streams of consequence and missed opportunities, you fall through the labyrinths of time searching for the rare path forward. Caught between always and every time at once, your anchor to the world \- your allies and your family \- is the last thing you have to hold onto. It's something worth fighting for, time after time.*

## **Temporal Jaunt**

	*Ripping through the fabric of space and time, you will have faced your future problems.*

	At 1st Rank, once per rest as an action, you may roll a d4 and record its outcome: you gain that number of lightspeed charges, and after that many rounds of combat end, your *past self* appears at a target unoccupied space within 30 feet of you (your *present self*) and may take a turn when your combat team takes initiative:

* **Identity.** Your past self and your present self both count as the same creature, except in two locations.

* **Causality.** If your past self becomes affected by any effect, your present self immediately becomes affected the same way. Your present self may be affected twice if both selves are caught by effects in an area or by being targeted as multiple creatures.

* **Now or Never.** All HP, ST, Spell Points, and usages of other features are shared by your past self and your present self.

* **Retroaction.** Your past self may take up to three actions that your present self would be able to take, but you must expend one lightspeed charge for each action taken in this manner.

* **Back to the Past.** Your past self disappears at the end of their turn.

## **Foreknowledge**

	*Your hazy premonitions are in search of your future, but you could just make one instead.*

	At 1st Rank, you learn the **Foreknowledge** lightform described below:

### *Foreknowledge*

## *Timetorn Evincer Lightform*

Immediately before you make a weapon attack check, you may expend a lightspeed charge to gain advantage on the check.

## **Isochronic Reprisal**

*Make good use of your time, even time-travel time.*

At 4th Rank, when you use **Vergence** while waiting for your past self to appear after using **Temporal Jaunt**, you may choose not to teleport. If you do, vergence effects still trigger as if you had teleported and as a vergence effect, you may remove one of the actions that will be granted by the **Retroaction** trait of **Temporal Jaunt** to immediately take an action of your choosing as if you were located in an unoccupied space within 5 feet of the target ally.

## **Paradoxical Loophole**

	*If you line up the timelines just right, maybe the universe won't notice.*

	At 8th Rank, if your present self and your past self use the same lightform at the same time, you expend the lightspeed charge cost once instead of twice.
