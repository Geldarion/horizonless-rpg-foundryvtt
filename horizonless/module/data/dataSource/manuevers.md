# List of Maneuvers

# **Acrobatics**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*Falling is a natural part of gymnastics.*

	You are able to take the **Handspring** and **Tuck and Roll** reactions described below:

* **Handspring.** As a reaction, when you cease being prone, you may travel up to half your Speed.
* **Tuck and Roll.** As a reaction, when you are dealt falling damage, you may halve the damage taken and travel up to half your Speed.

**Repetition Penalty:** The distance traveled taking either reaction becomes 5 feet.

**Advancement (+5 WIS and above):** You are now able to take the **Bounce Back** reaction as described below:

* **Bounce Back**. As a reaction, when you fall prone, you may cease being prone.

**Advancement (+5 CHA and above):** During movement performed by **Acrobatics**, you gain basic Jumping mobility.

# **Back to Back**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*No matter the odds, you've got each other's back.*

	As an action, if an ally within 5 feet of you is willing, both you and the ally become *back-to-back partners* to each other. Back-to-back partners remain so until they are further than 5 feet from each other. Back-to-back partners gain the following benefits:

* **Guarded.** You gain your Half Guard AC.

* **Together.** As a reaction, after your back-to-back partner is attacked, you may swap places with your back-to-back partner and make a light melee weapon attack against the attacker.

**Repetition Penalty:** Back-to-back partners from this maneuver do not gain the **Guarded** benefit.

**Advancement (+6 STR and above):** Back-to-back partners from this maneuver that gain the **Guarded** benefit also gain advantage on **Poise** saving throws.

**Advancement (+5 WIS and above):** After taking this manevuer's action, you may take a free Anticipate action.

**Advancement (+5 CHA and above):** If both back-to-back partners take the **Together** reaction during the same turn, the **Guarded** benefit instead grants them their Full Guard AC while they remain back-to-back partners.

# **Body Block**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*Heroically thrown into harm's way.*

	When you are targeted by an attack and you have a creature grappled, you may take a reaction to swap places with the creature you have grappled and cause the attack to target the grappled creature instead.

	Damage dealt by attacks retargeted by **Body Block** is halved.

**Repetition Penalty:** The creature becomes no longer grappled afterwards.

**Advancement (+6 STR and above):** Damage dealt by attacks repeated by this maneuver is no longer halved.

**Advancement (+5 WIS and above):** If a ranged attack's targeting path is within your Reach, and you are able to swing a creature you have grappled into the targeting path, you may take a reaction to do so and cause the attack to target the creature you have grappled instead.

# **Bull Rush**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*Take the fight with you wherever you go.*

	When you succeed on a Grapple check, you may perform this maneuver once per turn. If you do, once during this turn as a free action, you may travel up to your Speed.

**Repetition Penalty:** The distance you travel is halved.

**Advancement (+6 STR and above):** While performing this maneuver, you may act as if your Size were one category greater.

**Advancement (+6 DEX and above):** You ignore the effects of difficult terrain while traveling as part of performing this maneuver.

# **Clash**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*Their offense set on a collision course with yours.*

	As an action while using a melee fighting style, you prepare to *clash* against the first melee weapon attack made against you before the start of your next turn. When you clash, you make a special melee attack using your STR or DEX:

* **Push Back.** If your clash special attack check's total meets or exceeds the incoming attack check's total, the incoming attack misses.
* **Press Forward.** If your clash special attack check's total is greater than the incoming attack check's total by 5 or more, the incoming attack misses and you may immediately make a melee weapon attack against the attacker.

	If your clash special attack scores a critical hit, **Press Forward** occurs regardless of the total unless the incoming attack was also a critical hit.

**Repetition Penalty:** You incur disadvantage on the clash special attack check.

**Advancement (+6 STR and above):** If the attacker's Size is smaller than yours, you gain advantage on the clash special attack check. While using the Heavy fighting style, you may treat yourself as one Size category larger for this advancement.

**Advancement (+5 INT and above):** While you are prepared to clash, you may choose which incoming melee weapon attack to clash against, rather than automatically clashing against the first incoming melee weapon attack. You must choose to clash before the chosen attack check is rolled, and you may only clash once per performance of **Clash**.

**Advancement (+5 CHA and above):** If the attacker is a Boss creature, you gain advantage on the clash special attack check.

# **Collateral Shot**

## *Attack* *Maneuver*

*A precise, powerful shot punches through anything that dares stand in its way.*

	If the attack is a ranged weapon attack and the damage dealt causes a creature or object to either die, be knocked out, burn a Resolve, or break, the attack's targeting path is extended past the subject up to your fighting style's attack range until it intersects with a new one. If so, half of the damage dealt is dealt again to this new subject.

If the damage dealt to the new subject would activate **Collateral Shot**, you may repeat its effect, halving the damage dealt each time, though it may not affect subjects further than your fighting style's attack range.

**Repetition Penalty:** The attack check incurs disadvantage.

**Advancement (+6 STR and above):** The damage dealt is not halved each time this maneuver causes a creature or object to either die, be knocked out, expend a Resolve, or break.

**Advancement (+6 DEX and above):** If the targeting path intersects with a creature or non-obstruction object, you may skip over that subject and instead continue extending the targeting path until it intersects with a different subject.

# **Concussive Shock**

## *Attack* *Maneuver*

*Rung like a bell.*

	On a hit, if the attack is a melee weapon attack and the target is staggered, they become disoriented until the end of the current turn.

**Repetition Penalty:** The attack check incurs disadvantage.

**Advancement (+5 INT and above):** The disoriented condition persists until the start of the affected creature's turn.

# **Counterweighted Stance**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*Expertly balanced to really go the distance.*

	As an action, if you are using the Long fighting style, the range of your melee weapon attacks are increased by 5 feet until the end of combat or until you cease using the Long fighting style.

**Repetition Penalty:** You may not use this maneuver's listed advancements.

**Advancement (+6 DEX and above):** When you perform this maneuver, you may cease being prone.

**Advancement (+5 WIS and above):** When you perform this maneuver, you may take a free Anticipate action.

# **Create Door**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*Walls are discouragements you can soundly ignore.*

	As an action, you may travel up to your Speed. During this movement, you may take one free Wreck an Object action against an obstruction in the path of your travel.

**Repetition Penalty:** The distance you travel is halved.

**Advancement (+6 STR and above):** When you make a Wreck an Object check as part of this maneuver and you have a creature grappled, you gain advantage on the Wreck an Object check.

**Advancement (+6 DEX and above):** When you perform this maneuver, you may take an additional action. If you do, the distance you travel is doubled and you may take three free Wreck an Object checks against obstructions instead of one.

# **Curve Trajectory**

## *Attack* *Maneuver*

*The fletching catches the wind in just the right way to reach its mark.*

	If the attack is a ranged weapon attack, its targeting path may be bent at one point up to 90 degrees.

**Repetition Penalty:** The attack check incurs disadvantage.

**Advancement (+5 INT and above):** The maximum angle a bend can take becomes 180 degrees.

**Advancement (+5 CHA and above):** You may bend the targeting path at a second point up to 90 degrees (180 if you have the INT advancement).

# **Dagger Dance**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*There are more knives on your person than thought possible.*

	As an action, you may add half your Speed to your movement pool and gain *dagger dance* until the end of combat. While you have dagger dance, retrieving a weapon suitable for throwing is a free action.

**Repetition Penalty:** None

**Advancement (+6 DEX and above):** When you perform this maneuver, you may take a free Move action instead of adding half your Speed to your movement pool.

**Advancement (+5 CHA and above):** Once per turn while you have dagger dance, if you make three weapon attacks using the Thrown fighting style in a single turn, you may take a free Move action.

# **Danger Dive**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*We win together and we lose together.*

	As a reaction, when a target willing ally within your Reach makes a saving throw, you may add your Martial Die to the total.

If an ally fails a saving throw benefitting from your **Danger Dive**, you are also affected by any effects that occur from a failure.

**Repetition Penalty:** Afterwards, you and your target ally fall prone.

**Advancement (+5 WIS and above):** When you roll your Martial Die for this maneuver, you may roll twice and take the highest outcome.

**Advancement (+5 CHA and above):** If traveling up to your Speed towards an ally would qualify you to perform this maneuver, you may take a reaction to do so and then perform **Danger Dive** as part of the reaction.

# **Death from Above**

## *Attack* *Maneuver*

*A powerful plunge.*

	If the attack is a melee weapon attack and is made during or immediately after falling further than 20 feet, the falling damage you take is halved, the attack check gains advantage, and the attack check's critical hit threshold is reduced by 2\.

**Repetition Penalty:** The attack check incurs disadvantage.

**Advancement (+6 STR and above):** On a hit, if the attack qualifies for **Death from Above**, you fell 30 feet or more, and the target is your Size or smaller, the target falls prone.

**Advancement (+5 INT and above):** The critical hit threshold reduction becomes 3\.

**Advancement (+5 WIS and above):** The fall distance required to perform this maneuver is reduced to 10 feet.

# **Devastating Blow**

## *Attack* *Maneuver*

*You really throw your weight into this one.*

	If the attack is made while using the Heavy fighting style, it gains the following properties:

* **Wind Up.** The attack check's total is reduced by 3\.

* **Tear Down.** On a hit, the outcomes of all of your Martial Die rolled for the attack's damage roll are their maximum value.

**Repetition Penalty:** The attack check incurs disadvantage.

**Advancement (+6 STR and above):** On a hit with a qualifying attack, the target is staggered.

**Advancement (+5 INT and above):** If a qualifying attack targets a staggered creature, you ignore the **Wind Up** property of this maneuver.

# **Disarm**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*Let go of those violent urges.*

	As an action, if you have a creature grappled with no Resolve remaining, you may force that creature to drop everything they are holding.

**Repetition Penalty:** The creature must also be restrained by you to perform this maneuver.

**Advancement (+6 STR and above):** When you succeed on a Pin check, you may perform this maneuver as a free action.

**Advancement (+6 DEX and above):** As a reaction, when you cause a creature to drop an object from this maneuver, you may catch that object.

# **Drop Shot**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*The perfect shot requires perfect timing.*

	If a ranged attack is made by a subject other than you, whose targeting path intersects with a space you occupy, you may take a free action to fall prone and grant the attack check advantage.

**Repetition Penalty:** The attack check no longer gains advantage and no advancements take effect.

**Advancement (+6 DEX and above):** After falling prone from this maneuver, you may take a reaction to cease being prone.

**Advancement (+5 CHA and above):** In addition to gaining advantage, the attack's critical hit threshold is reduced by 1\.

# **Enveloppement**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*"Taking the steel" is sometimes literal.*

When you use the **Offhand Attack** trait of the Dual Wielding fighting style, you may replace the light weapon attack with a melee special attack using your STR or DEX against a target within your Reach. On a hit against a target with no Resolve remaining, the target must drop an object they are holding of your choosing.

**Repetition Penalty:** You incur disadvantage on the special attack check.

**Advancement (+6 DEX and above):** If the target drops an object from this maneuver, the target must drop everything they are holding.

**Advancement (+5 CHA and above):** If the target drops an object from this maneuver, the target incurs disadvantage on all weapon attack checks they make until the start of your next turn.

# **Eviscerate**

## *Attack* *Maneuver*

*When the opponent isn't ready for it, you know how to make it quick.*

	If the target is staggered, the attack's critical hit threshold is reduced by 2\.

**Repetition Penalty:** The attack check incurs disadvantage.

**Advancement (+6 STR and above):** The critical hit threshold reduction becomes 3\.

**Advancement (+5 INT and above):** If the attack is a critical hit, the critical hit threshold reduction of this maneuver is applied to all attacks you make against the target until the end of the current turn.

# **Explosive Entrance**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*Kick down doors first, ask questions later.*

	If you make an Initiate check, your combat team is not ambushed, and you enter the combat with a loud and bombastic entrance (such as kicking down a door), you may add your Martial Die to your Initiate check's total.

**Repetition Penalty:** None

**Advancement (+6 STR and above):** Wreck an Object checks made as part of your loud and bombastic entrance gain advantage.

**Advancement (+5 CHA and above):** So long as you are the instigator of the combat, all entrances you make going into combat are considered sufficiently bombastic to use **Explosive Entrance**.

# **Fastball Special**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*Who needs range when you've got your best friends around you*

	When you take the Throw action, you may act as if a target willing ally within your Reach was grappled by you and throw them instead. If you do, the distance thrown is doubled.

**Repetition Penalty:** The distance thrown is not doubled by this maneuver and the STR advancement does not occur.

**Advancement (+6 STR and above):** The distance thrown by this maneuver is doubled again.

**Advancement (+5 CHA and above):** If the target ally is your initiative partner, they gain advantage on the next attack check they make this turn.

# **Finisher**

## *Attack* *Maneuver*

*End of the line.*

	If the target has no Resolve remaining, the attack's critical hit threshold is reduced by 2\. If the attack scores a critical hit, dice rolled for the damage roll is tripled rather than doubled.

**Repetition Penalty:** The attack check incurs disadvantage.

**Advancement (+5 INT and above):** The critical hit threshold reduction becomes 3\.

**Advancement (+5 CHA and above):** If the target has no Resolve remaining, the attack check additionally gains advantage.

# **Flanking Assault**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*Attack from all angles, all at once.*

	If a hostile creature is between you and your initiative partner, each of you may take an action to make a light melee weapon attack against the creature. If both attacks hit, the target becomes staggered.

**Repetition Penalty:** Both attack checks incur disadvantage.

**Advancement (+5 INT and above):** If your initiative partner attacks first and the attack hits, you gain advantage on your attack.

**Advancement (+5 CHA and above):** Your initiative partner gains advantage on their attack check.

# **Fling**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*Keeping them at arm's length is nowhere close to enough.*

	When you take the Throw action or throw a creature into another creature as an attack, you may double the distance thrown.

**Repetition Penalty:** The distance thrown is instead increased by 5 feet.

**Advancement (+6 STR and above):** When a creature is thrown into an obstruction while performing this maneuver, you may take a free Wreck an Object action against that obstruction.

**Advancement (+5 WIS and above):** While performing this maneuver, if you are not incurring this maneuver's repetition penalty and the only creatures within your Reach are creatures you are throwing, you may double the distance thrown an additional time.

# **Goading Remark**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*Is that really the best you can do*

	As an action, you may cause a target creature within 30 feet of you to make a **Courage** saving throw. On a failure, the target is provoked by you until they hit you with an attack or until combat ends.

**Repetition Penalty:** The target gains advantage on the **Courage** saving throw.

**Advancement (+5 WIS and above):** You gain DB equal to 5   your Tier Bonus against damage dealt by creatures you have provoked by this maneuver (including the damage dealt by the attack that ends the effect).

**Advancement (+5 CHA and above):** Creatures you have provoked by this maneuver are unable to willingly move away from you.

# **Hooking Trip**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*Hook, line, and sinker.*

	When you take the Trip action while using the Long fighting style, you may act as if your Reach was your weapon attack range for the action.

**Repetition Penalty:** You incur disadvantage on the Trip check.

**Advancement (+6 STR and above):** This maneuver may be applied to Shove actions in a manner similar to Trip actions. The repetition penalty additionally imposes disadvantage on Shove checks.

# **Intercept**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*With quick reflexes, you step in to bat away an attack with your shield.*

	As a reaction, while you are gaining the benefits of the Raise Shield action and an ally within your Reach is targeted by a melee attack, you may force the attack to target you instead.

Attacks retargeted by **Intercept** allow the attacker to treat you as being at the ally's location for the attack.

**Repetition Penalty:** After performing this maneuver, you cease gaining the benefits of the Raise Shield action.

**Advancement (+5 WIS and above):** You may use **Intercept** for any type of attack.

**Advancement (+5 CHA and above):** If traveling up to your Speed towards an ally would qualify you to perform this maneuver, you may take a reaction to do so and then perform **Intercept** as part of the reaction.

# **Long Shot**

## *Attack* *Maneuver*

*Past the rolling hills and beyond the valley, your aim holds true.*

	If the attack is a ranged weapon attack, the attack ignores the disadvantage imposed by attacking at long range.

**Repetition Penalty:** The attack check incurs disadvantage.

**Advancement (+6 DEX and above):** If you are attacking at long range and you are not incurring the repetition penalty, the attack gains advantage.

**Advancement (+5 WIS and above):** You may add 15 feet to the attack's range and 30 feet to the attack's long range.

# **Menacing**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*Your display of martial prowess dovetails into a strikingly menacing pose.*

	As an action, you may cause a target creature within 30 feet of you to make a **Courage** saving throw. On a failure, an affected creature is frightened of you for 10 minutes. While frightened by this maneuver, a creature may retry the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success.

**Repetition Penalty:** An affected creature gains advantage on the initial **Courage** saving throw.

**Advancement (+5 INT and above):** When an affected creature fails the initial **Courage** saving throw, all of their retries on the saving throw incur disadvantage.

**Advancement (+5 WIS and above):** When you cause a creature to die, be knocked out, or burn a Resolve, you may perform **Menacing** as a reaction.

**Advancement (+5 CHA and above):** You may target any number of creatures within 30 feet of you for this maneuver.

# **Overpower**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*An unprepared foe is easily bested.*

	If you take the Grapple action, the target is staggered, and the target is one Size category larger than you or smaller, you may remove the condition to automatically succeed on the Grapple check.

**Repetition Penalty:** Rather than automatically succeeding, you instead gain advantage on the Grapple check.

**Advancement (+6 STR and above):** While the target is grappled by you as a result of performing this maneuver, you gain advantage on Pin checks against them.

# **Parry**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*A flash of steel separates you from harm.*

	When a melee weapon attack is made against you while using the Dual Wielding, Improvised, Solitary, or Unarmed fighting styles, you may take a reaction to gain your Full Guard AC for the attack.

**Repetition Penalty:** You instead gain your Half Guard AC if you do not already have it.

**Advancement (+6 DEX and above):** After causing an attack to miss with this maneuver, you may take an additional reaction to make a melee weapon attack against the attacker.

**Advancement (+5 WIS and above):** You may use **Parry** against any type of attack.

**Advancement (+5 CHA and above):** The benefits of this maneuver persist until the end of the current turn.

# **Pierce**

## *Attack* *Maneuver*

*This. Is. Going. To. HIT.*

	If the target is staggered, their AC is reduced to their Base AC for the attack.

**Repetition Penalty:** The attack check incurs disadvantage.

**Advancement (+5 INT and above):** Once per round on a hit, if the target would have otherwise been using their Full Guard AC, the target becomes staggered again.

**Advancement (+5 CHA and above):** On a hit, the target's AC is reduced to their Base AC for all attacks made against them until the end of the current turn.

# **Point Blank**

## *Attack* *Maneuver*

*Arguably the easiest to line up.*

	If you are using either the Loading Ranged, Skirmisher, or Thrown fighting styles and the attack is a ranged weapon attack, you ignore the disadvantage on ranged attacks imposed by a hostile creature being able to target you with a melee weapon attack.

**Repetition Penalty:** The attack check incurs disadvantage.

**Advancement (+6 DEX and above):** If the attack qualifies for **Point Blank** and you are affected by the **Constrained** condition trait such as by being prone or restrained, you may ignore the disadvantage imposed by the trait for the attack.

**Advancement (+5 CHA and above):** If the attack qualifies for **Point Blank** and you are affected by the **Repelled** condition trait such as by being frightened, you may ignore the disadvantage imposed by the trait for the attack.

# **Pressure Point**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*Suddenly, it hits them.*

	When you make an Analyze check, if you are able to take the Stagger action against the target of the Analyze check, you may immediately do so as a free action.

**Repetition Penalty:** You incur disadvantage on both the Analyze check and the Stagger attack check.

**Advancement (+6 DEX and above):** You may travel up to 10 feet towards the target of the Analyze check before taking the free Stagger action.

**Advancement (+5 INT and above):** If you succeed on the Analyze check and hit a target that was already staggered, you may replace the staggered condition you impose with being disoriented until the end of the current turn.

# **Priming Shot**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*A grazing blow to create an opening.*

	When you take the Stagger action while using a ranged fighting style, you may extend the range of the Stagger attack to the range of your fighting style weapon attacks. If you do this while using the Loading Ranged fighting style, this unloads the weapon.

**Repetition Penalty:** The Stagger attack check incurs disadvantage.

**Advancement (+5 WIS and above):** You may extend the range of the Stagger attack to your fighting style's long range and incur disadvantage on the attack check.

# **Quick Fix**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*Walk it off.*

	As an action, you may grant a target creature within your Reach other than you the following benefits:

* The target **regains Xd4 \+ X ST**, where X is your Tier Bonus.
* If the target is affected by effects with the **Recoverable** or **Out of It** condition traits, such as being ablaze, prone, or unconscious, those effects end.

**Repetition Penalty:** The ST regained from this maneuver is halved.

**Advancement (+5 INT and above):** Creatures targeted by this maneuver may immediately retry saving throws against effects that allow taking an action to retry their saving throw.

**Advancement (+5 WIS and above):** The minimum ST recovered from this maneuver is now 2  your Tier Bonus.

**Advancement (+5 CHA and above):** You may target an additional creature within your Reach.

# **Read and Repeat**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*Predictable, every time.*

	As a reaction immediately after a creature or object you **perceive** misses an attack against you, you may take a free Dodge action.

**Repetition Penalty:** You are unable to perform this maneuver.

**Advancement (+6 DEX and above):** You may replace the free Dodge action with a free Vanish action.

**Advancement (+5 WIS and above):** You only need to be **aware** of an attacker to perform **Read and Repeat**.

# **Sentinel Strike**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*Even amidst the rapids of battle, you catch the currents.*

	While using a melee fighting style, when a creature or object travels into, out of, or through a space within the attack range of your fighting style, you may take a reaction to make a light melee weapon attack against the creature or object.

**Repetition Penalty:** The attack check incurs disadvantage.

**Advancement (+6 STR and above):** You may replace the light melee weapon attack with a free Grapple action against the same target. The repetition penalty attack disadvantage is instead imposed on the Grapple check.

**Advancement (+6 DEX and above):** You may replace the light melee weapon attack with a melee weapon attack against the same target.

**Advancement (+5 WIS and above):** While using a melee fighting style, you may also perform this maneuver as a reaction when a creature or object casts a spell or gets up from being prone while within your fighting style's attack range.

# **Shield Bash**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*Offense and defense are just two sides of the same shield.*

	When you take the Raise Shield action, you may take a free Stagger action.

**Repetition Penalty:** Taking the Raise Shield action may only grant your Half Guard AC. You incur disadvantage on the Stagger attack check.

**Advancement (+6 STR and above):** You may replace the free Stagger action with a free Shove or Trip action. The repetition penalty attack disadvantage is instead imposed on the chosen STR combat check.

# **Speedload**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*Like a well-oiled machine.*

	As a reaction during your turn, you may take a free Reload object interaction.

**Repetition Penalty:** You incur disadvantage on the next weapon attack check you make before the end of your next turn.

**Advancement (+6 DEX and above):** You may perform **Speedload** as a reaction at any point.

# **Strike Through**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*In the blink of an eye, you cut through the front line, spelling their demise with the sheathing of your blade.*

	While using the Solitary fighting style, you may take two actions to travel up to half your Speed to an unoccupied space and then make a melee weapon attack against each creature whose occupied space you traveled through. During this movement, you are able to travel through the spaces occupied by creatures of any Size and do so without treating those spaces as difficult terrain.

**Repetition Penalty:** All attack checks incur disadvantage.

**Advancement (+6 STR and above):** You may replace a weapon attack with a free Shove action against the same target.

**Advancement (+6 DEX and above):** All weapon attacks except the first made as part of this maneuver ignore weapon attack fatigue.

# **Sundering Bolt**

## *Attack* *Maneuver*

*When the final hour strikes, there will be no tomorrow.*

	If you are using the Loading Ranged or Skirmisher fighting style, the attack is a ranged weapon attack, and it scores a critical hit, you may add all of the dice rolled in the damage roll an additional time (in the absence of other features, dice are tripled rather than doubled).

**Repetition Penalty:** The attack check incurs disadvantage.

**Advancement (+5 INT and above):** If the attack scores a critical hit and causes the target to burn a Resolve, they become staggered afterwards.

**Advancement (+5 WIS and above):** The attack's critical hit threshold is reduced by 1\.

# **Tackle**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*Run into every problem head-first.*

	As an action, you may travel up to your Speed towards a target creature. If the target is within your Reach at the end of this movement, you may take a free Shove or Trip action against it.

**Repetition Penalty:** The distance you travel is halved.

**Advancement (+6 STR and above):** When you push a target with a Shove check while performing this maneuver, you may double the distance the target is pushed.

**Advancement (+5 CHA and above):** When you cause a target to fall prone from a Trip check while performing this maneuver, the target's Speed is halved until the end of their next turn.

# **Tactical Repositioning**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*You create a daring opening, allowing an ally to get into position.*

	As an action, if your initiative partner is within 30 feet of you, you may allow them to travel up to half their Speed.

**Repetition Penalty:** The distance traveled by this maneuver becomes 5 feet.

**Advancement (+5 WIS and above):** You may affect your initiative partner with **Tactical Repositioning** up to 60 feet away from you.

**Advancement (+5 CHA and above):** Instead of affecting your initiative partner with this maneuver, you may instead choose an ally within 30 feet of you. If you have this maneuver's WIS enhancement, this distance also becomes 60 feet.

# **Thread the Needle**

## *Attack* *Maneuver*

*In a scarce collection of seconds you find the exact shot that counts.*

If you are using the Basic Ranged fighting style, you may choose to take one, two, or no *drawing actions* immediately before making the attack. The attack gains benefits according to the number of drawing actions taken:

* **No Drawing Actions.** The critical hit threshold is reduced by a total of 1\.

* **One Drawing Action.** The critical hit threshold is reduced by a total of 3\.

* **Two Drawing Actions.** The critical hit threshold is reduced by a total of 5\.

**Repetition Penalty:** The attack check incurs disadvantage.

**Advancement (+6 DEX and above):** If you take at least one drawing action, the attack gains advantage.

**Advancement (+5 INT and above):** If the attack scores a critical hit, the target becomes staggered.

# **Tie Them Up**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*Rope is optional but can help in situations where their skeleton doesn't allow for knots.*

When you make a Pin check, you may perform this maneuver. Afterwards, creatures you currently have restrained by a successful Pin check no longer need to be grappled by you to remain restrained. Those creatures remain restrained until they succeed on an Escape check against you (they may take Escape actions as if they were still grappled by you).

**Repetition Penalty:** You incur disadvantage on the Pin check.

**Advancement (+6 DEX and above):** After performing this maneuver, if you did not incur its repetition penalty, you may travel 10 feet.

**Advancement (+5 CHA and above):** When a creature ceases being restrained from this maneuver, they fall prone afterwards.

# **Under Pressure**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*When it comes to being in the right place at the right time, you are fashionably late.*

	When you take the Move action while you have no ST, you may add your Speed to your movement pool an additional time.

**Repetition Penalty:** The additional movement from this maneuver becomes 10 feet.

**Advancement (+5 WIS and above):** While spending movement granted while performing **Under Pressure** (including the Move action), you ignore difficult terrain.

**Advancement (+5 CHA and above):** After performing this maneuver, you may **regain ST** equal to your Level.

# **Volley**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*You pin down the enemy with a barrage of arrows.*

	As two actions, while using the Basic Ranged fighting style, you designate a 10 foot radius sphere originating from a target location within your fighting style's range that persists until the start of your next turn. Whenever a creature you can target is in the area for the first time since the start of your most recent turn, you may cause them to make a basic **Reflex** saving throw to resist **Xd8 damage**, where X is your Tier Bonus. The type of damage dealt matches what your weapon would deal.

**Repetition Penalty:** All affected creatures gain advantage on the **Reflex** saving throw imposed.

**Advancement (+6 DEX and above):** The radius of the area becomes 20 feet.

**Advancement (+5 WIS and above):** The area may originate from a target location within your fighting style's long range.

**Advancement (+5 CHA and above):** If a creature fails the **Reflex** saving throw, they treat the area as difficult terrain while it persists.

# **Wallop**

## *Attack* *Maneuver*

*Push comes to shove.*

	On a hit, if the attack is a melee weapon attack and the target is staggered, you may either push them 5 feet away from you or cause them to fall prone.

**Repetition Penalty:** The attack check incurs disadvantage.

**Advancement (+6 STR and above):** If you choose to push the target, the distance pushed becomes 10 feet.

**Advancement (+5 INT and above):** You may choose to both push the target and cause them to fall prone.

# **Whirlwind**

## *Strategy* *Maneuver*

*With blinding speed, you strike out at any and all unfortunate enough to find themselves near you.*

	You may take three actions to make up to eight melee weapon attacks. Each attack ignores weapon attack fatigue and must be against a different creature or object.

**Repetition Penalty:** All attack checks incur disadvantage.

**Advancement (+6 STR and above):** The maximum number of attacks made becomes twelve.

**Advancement (+6 DEX and above):** You may travel up to 5 feet before making each melee weapon attack.
